PURPOSE         To ensure the accuracy and proper reporting under local, state and federal
                regulations of data concerning work-related fatalities, injuries and illnesses
                recorded by different commands.

PROCEDURE When a work related fatality, injury or illness occurs:

MEMBER OF       1.      Notify supervisor promptly.
THE             2.      Report fatality, injury or illness as outlined in appropriate Patrol Guide or
SERVICE                 Administrative Guide procedures etc.
                3.      Forward information concerning the work-related fatality, injury, or illness
                        to command safety officer.

SUPERVISOR 4.           Notify Medical Division Sick Desk immediately by phone and email at
                        sickdesk@nypd.org when the following occurs:
                        a.     Death of one or more members of the service and/or
                        b.     In-patient hospitalization of two or more members of the service,
                               resulting from the same incident, and/or
                        c.     In-patient hospitalization of one or more members of the service, a
                               member's amputation, or loss of a member's eye.

MEDICAL         5.      Notify New York State Department of Labor within eight hours by phone
DIVISION                and email when the following occurs:
SICK DESK               a.     Death of one or more members of the service and/or
                        b.     In-patient hospitalization of two or more members of the service,
                               resulting from the same incident.
                6.      Notify New York State Department of Labor within 24 hours by phone and
                        email when the following occurs:
                        a.     In-patient hospitalization of one or more members of the service, a
                               member's amputation, or loss of a member's eye.
