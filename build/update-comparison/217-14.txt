PURPOSE             To ensure that the appropriate reference marker numerals/letters are utilized
                    when preparing a Police Crash Report (MV-104P).

PROCEDURE          Upon notification or observation of a vehicle collision occurring on limited
                    access highways, collector-distributor roadways (CDRs), entrance and exit
                    ramps, and bridges and tunnels (Metropolitan Transportation Authority facilities,
                    Brooklyn and Williamsburg Bridges, etc.):
