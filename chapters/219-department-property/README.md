# Section 219 — Department Property

## Source
- **Patrol Guide Section:** 219
- **Source PDF:** public-pguide4.pdf (OCR version)
- **Priority:** Low

## Learning Objectives
- Know the vehicle inspection requirements (daily and weekly) and required equipment
- Understand fuel dispensing card types and delivery supervision procedures
- Know the reporting chain and notifications for lost/stolen Department property, firearms, shields, and ID cards
- Identify rules for IT systems security, portable radios, LPR devices, and mobile digital devices
- Understand the eligibility requirements and restrictions for Restricted Parking Permits
- Know the rules governing OMNY Police Passes, E-Z Pass tags, and radiation detection pagers
- Recognize emergency power outage procedures and fuel tank responsibilities

## Sergeant Focus Areas
- Vehicle inspection accountability (daily tour checks + weekly Sunday inspections)
- Notification chain for lost/stolen property: Desk Officer → CO/Ops Coordinator → IAB + Stolen Property Inquiry Unit
- Disciplinary consultation with Department Advocate's Office for lost/stolen firearms
- ICO duties: monthly DAS audits (5 members), E-Z Pass monitoring, OMNY pass inspections, parking permit background checks
- LPR alarm response: ALWAYS verify through NYSPIN before law enforcement action
- Emergency power: disconnect non-essential equipment from red-plate receptacles; quarterly room surveys
- Restricted Parking Permit: CO has final authority; 5+ red light or 15+ school speed violations = ineligible
