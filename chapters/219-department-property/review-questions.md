# Section 219 — Review Questions

## Instructions

Select the best answer for each question. Answers are provided after each question.

---

**1.** Under P.G. 219-08, tire chains should NOT be installed on Department vehicles until there is more than how many inches of snow?

A) 2 inches
B) 3 inches
C) 4 inches
D) 6 inches

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**C) 4 inches**

Tire chains are NOT installed until there is more than 4 inches of snow. Additionally, tire chains are NOT installed on front-wheel drive vehicles. (P.G. 219-08)

</details>

---

**2.** Which color fuel card is designated for the CRALO?

A) Green
B) White
C) Yellow
D) Orange

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**C) Yellow**

Card colors: Green = Master, Yellow = CRALO, Orange = Non-Fleet, Red = Private, White = Operator. (P.G. 219-11)

</details>

---

**3.** How often must the Integrity Control Officer audit the Domain Awareness System (DAS) and how many members must be reviewed?

A) Weekly, 3 random members
B) Monthly, 5 random members
C) Quarterly, 10 random members
D) Semi-annually, 5 random members

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**B) Monthly, 5 random members**

The ICO conducts a monthly DAS audit reviewing 5 random members. (P.G. 219-14)

</details>

---

**4.** If a portable radio is lost and the member was NOT carrying it in an authorized carrying case, the finding will be:

A) No fault
B) Accidental loss
C) Negligence
D) Misconduct

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**C) Negligence**

If a portable radio is lost and was not in an authorized carrying case at the time, the member is deemed negligent. (P.G. 219-15)

</details>

---

**5.** Which of the following is NOT a required notification when a portable radio is lost or stolen?

A) Operations Unit
B) Electronics Section
C) Fleet Services Division
D) Stolen Property Inquiry Unit

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**C) Fleet Services Division**

The four required notifications are: Operations Unit, Electronics Section, Internal Affairs Bureau, and Stolen Property Inquiry Unit. Fleet Services Division is not notified for radio losses. (P.G. 219-15)

</details>

**6.** Weekly vehicle inspections are conducted on what day and after which platoon has been posted?

A) Saturday, after the first platoon
B) Sunday, after the second platoon
C) Monday, after the first platoon
D) Friday, after the third platoon

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**B) Sunday, after the second platoon**

The lieutenant platoon commander/patrol supervisor inspects all Department vehicles each Sunday after the second platoon has been posted. (P.G. 219-02)

</details>

**7.** Regulated infectious waste from patrol kits is picked up by:

A) Department of Sanitation
B) Medical Division messenger
C) FDNY Hazmat
D) Quartermaster Section

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**B) Medical Division messenger**

The Medical Division messenger picks up regulated infectious waste from the 32-gallon biohazard receptacle at the command. (P.G. 219-17)

</details>

**8.** Before a law enforcement action is initiated based on a License Plate Reader (LPR) alarm, the operator must:

A) Notify the patrol supervisor
B) Contact the Real Time Crime Center
C) Verify the vehicle status through NYSPIN
D) Photograph the license plate

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**C) Verify the vehicle status through NYSPIN**

It is absolutely essential that the LPR operator verify the current status of a vehicle through NYSPIN prior to initiating any law enforcement action. The LPR is NOT connected to NYSPIN and activates on partial scan matches. (P.G. 219-31)

</details>

**9.** NYCT NYPD OMNY Police Passes are NOT valid for use on which type of transit?

A) Subway
B) Local bus
C) MTA express bus
D) Staten Island Railway

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**C) MTA express bus**

OMNY Police Passes are not valid for use on MTA express buses. Besides this exception, they have no electronic restrictions and may be used on subways and public buses. (P.G. 219-26)

</details>

---

**10.** A vehicle with how many red light camera violations within a twelve-month period will be ineligible for a Restricted Parking Permit?

A) 3 or more
B) 5 or more
C) 10 or more
D) 15 or more

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**B) 5 or more**

Vehicles with 5 or more red light camera violations OR 15 or more school speed camera violations within a twelve-month period are ineligible. (P.G. 219-29)

</details>

---

**11.** Commands with a single fuel tank (heating and generator) must maintain the fuel level above what percentage?

A) 25%
B) 50%
C) 75%
D) 90%

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**B) 50%**

Commands with a single fuel tank must maintain the level above 50%. Commands with two separate tanks are responsible only for the heating tank; Building Maintenance Section maintains the generator tank. (P.G. 219-28)

</details>

---

**12.** When a member's firearm is lost and disciplinary action is warranted, the commanding officer must consult with:

A) Internal Affairs Bureau
B) Legal Bureau
C) Department Advocate's Office
D) Chief of Department

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**C) Department Advocate's Office**

When disciplinary action is warranted for loss of or failure to safeguard a firearm, a consultation with the Department Advocate's Office is required to determine appropriate disciplinary action. (P.G. 219-21)

</details>

---

**13.** A member's firearm, shield, or ID card is lost within Police Headquarters (5th Precinct). The investigation will be conducted by:

A) The 5th Precinct commanding officer
B) A captain or above from the reporting member's command
C) The Headquarters Security Unit
D) Internal Affairs Bureau

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**B) A captain or above from the reporting member's command**

At Police Headquarters or Police Academy, the loss is reported to the Headquarters Security Unit (or Police Academy Integrity Unit), but the investigation is conducted by a captain or above from the reporting member's command. (P.G. 219-21)

</details>

---

**14.** Which of the following is a permitted use of a Department tow vehicle?

A) Towing a civilian vehicle to a repair shop
B) Towing an inoperable Department vehicle
C) Removing vehicles for alternate side parking
D) Towing abandoned civilian vehicles to the pound

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**B) Towing an inoperable Department vehicle**

Department Tow has limited authorized uses: inoperable Department vehicle, evidence vehicle, parade/emergency path clearance, assisting ESU, and lifting heavy articles. (P.G. 219-09)

</details>

---

**15.** How many business days prior to foreign travel must a member request ITB Information Security Desk permission to bring a Department mobile device?

A) 2 business days
B) 3 business days
C) 5 business days
D) 10 business days

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**C) 5 business days**

Members must request permission from the ITB Information Security Desk at least 5 business days prior to taking a Department mobile digital device to a foreign country. (P.G. 219-32)

</details>
