## P.G. 219-17: Patrol Kits (Personal Protective Equipment)

**Date Effective:** 12/31/24 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 78 | **Pages:** 2

### Purpose

To ensure patrol kits containing personal protective equipment (PPE) are maintained and available.

### Patrol Kit Contents

| Item |
|------|
| Disposable gowns |
| Eye/mouth shields |
| Heavy duty gloves |
| Shoe covers |
| Antiseptic wipes |
| Biohazard bag |

### Biohazard Waste

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Receptacle | **32-gallon** biohazard receptacle at command |
| Pickup | **Medical Division messenger** picks up regulated infectious waste |

> **Exam Alert:** Regulated infectious waste is picked up by the **Medical Division messenger**, not sanitation or other units.

## P.G. 219-25: E-Z Pass Tags

**Date Effective:** 12/31/24 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 78 | **Pages:** 2

### Purpose

To account for the safekeeping and usage of E-Z Pass tags assigned to each command.

### Responsibilities

| Role | Duty |
|------|------|
| CO | Ensure lieutenant/patrol supervisor accounts for all tags |
| CO | Have ICO monitor E-Z Pass usage |
| Lt. Platoon Cmdr/Patrol Supervisor | Inspect all vehicles each Sunday after second platoon; verify tags affixed to center top windshield; record serial numbers on VEHICLE INSPECTION REPORT |
| ICO | Maintain inventory; review usage for compliance; incorporate into monthly self-inspection |

### Mounting Rules

| Vehicle Type | Mounting Method |
|-------------|----------------|
| Standard | **Permanently affixed** with double-sided tape (top center windshield) |
| Vehicles routinely leaving city (e.g., Category I) | Secured with **velcro strips** (removable) |

### Transfer Restrictions

- Commands are **NOT authorized** to transfer E-Z Pass tags between vehicles
- All reassignments approved by CO, Fleet Services Division

### Authorized Toll Crossings (Partial List)

Bronx-Whitestone Bridge, George Washington Bridge, Bayonne Bridge, Goethals Bridge, Henry Hudson Bridge, Holland Tunnel, Queens Midtown Tunnel, Lincoln Tunnel, Throgs Neck Bridge, Outerbridge Crossing, Cross Bay Veterans Memorial Bridge, Verrazano-Narrows Bridge, Hugh L. Carey Tunnel, Robert F. Kennedy Bridge, Marine Parkway-Gil Hodges Memorial Bridge

### NOT Authorized Toll Crossings (Partial List)

New Jersey Turnpike, New York State Thruway, Garden State Parkway, New England Thruway, Governor Mario M. Cuomo (Tappan Zee) Bridge

### Lost/Stolen E-Z Pass

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Notify desk officer |
| 2 | Prepare COMPLAINT REPORT |
| 3 | Notify IAB; enter log number on report |
| 4 | Notify Stolen Property Inquiry Unit of serial number |
| 5 | CO investigates; forwards report to CO, Fleet Services Division |

> **Exam Alert:** E-Z Pass tags are **NOT authorized** for the NJ Turnpike, NY State Thruway, Garden State Parkway, New England Thruway, or Tappan Zee Bridge.

> **Exam Alert:** Commands may **NOT** transfer E-Z Pass tags between vehicles — Fleet Services Division must approve.

## P.G. 219-26: NYCT NYPD OMNY Police Passes

**Date Effective:** 01/06/26 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 3 | **Pages:** 4

### Purpose

To provide for the issuance, control, and strict accountability of NYCT NYPD OMNY Police Passes to all uniformed members.

### Key Facts

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Issued by | Human Resources Division |
| Assigned to | Individual uniformed member (NOT the command) |
| Unique serial number | Each pass has its own number |
| Validity period | Generally **7 years** |
| Renewal | Coordinated by Shield, ID and Uniformed Services Unit |
| Carry requirement | At all times when shield and ID card required |
| Not valid on | **MTA express buses** |
| Usage monitoring | Each use **electronically recorded** by NYC Transit |

### CO Responsibilities

| Duty |
|------|
| Maintain account of all passes |
| Ensure all uniformed members have a valid pass |
| Enter serial number on member's FORCE RECORD |
| Have ICO monitor the program |
| Investigate damaged/defective passes |
| Investigate lost/stolen passes per P.G. 219-20 |

### ICO Responsibilities

| Duty |
|------|
| Enter serial number on FORCE RECORD |
| Inspect passes at roll calls and semi-annual uniform inspections |
| Investigate damaged/defective passes; prepare Typed Letterhead report |
| Hand deliver damaged/defective pass to Shield, ID and Uniformed Services Unit |

### Long-Term Leave

- Member must **surrender** OMNY pass immediately prior to long-term leave (military, child care)
- Pass hand-delivered to Shield, ID and Uniformed Services Unit
- Upon return, report prepared requesting re-issuance

### Lost/Stolen Procedure

| Step | Role | Action |
|------|------|--------|
| 1 | Member | Notify desk officer |
| 2 | Desk Officer | Notify CO and operations coordinator |
| 3 | Operations Coordinator | Comply with P.G. 219-20; prepare COMPLAINT REPORT |
| 4 | Operations Coordinator | Notify IAB Command Center; enter log number |
| 5 | Operations Coordinator | Notify Stolen Property Inquiry Unit |
| 6 | Operations Coordinator | Notify **Transit Bureau Wheel** and Shield, ID and Uniformed Services Unit |

### Separation from Service

- All passes surrendered upon separation (retirement, resignation, dismissal)
- Notation on PROPERTY RECEIPT — DISCONTINUANCE OF SERVICE (PD520-013)
- Pass hand-delivered to Shield, ID and Uniformed Services Unit

> **Exam Alert:** OMNY passes are NOT valid on **MTA express buses**. All other subway/bus use is permitted.

> **Exam Alert:** Passes are assigned to the **individual member**, NOT the command. Commands may NOT assign or reassign passes.

> **Exam Alert:** Unauthorized person using an OMNY pass can be charged with **theft of service** and **impersonation of a Police Officer**.

## P.G. 219-27: Radiation Detection Pagers — Command-Assigned

**Date Effective:** 11/18/21 | **Last Revision:** 1.0.110 | **Pages:** 3

### Purpose

To provide for control, accountability, and issuance of radiation detection pagers assigned to commands.

### Key Points

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Assigned by | Counterterrorism Central Resource Division through Citywide Counterterrorism Coordinator |
| Training | **ONLY trained** supervisors/members may use pagers |
| Battery type | **AAA** batteries (obtained from Quartermaster Section) |
| Deployment | Every tour — pager(s) assigned to trained patrol supervisor/member |
| Transfer | Commands NOT authorized to transfer pagers to other commands |
| Inspection | Desk officer inspects/accounts for all pagers at start of each tour |
| Command Log | Serial numbers and operating status recorded |
| If alarm activates | Comply with P.G. 212-102 |
| Lost/stolen | Process per P.G. 219-20; notify Citywide Counterterrorism Office immediately |

### Repair/Replacement

- Desk officer investigates and prepares Typed Letterhead report
- Contact **Patrol Borough Counterterrorism Coordinator** for return/replacement instructions

## P.G. 219-29: NYPD Restricted Parking Permits

**Date Effective:** 05/25/22 | **Last Revision:** 1.0.64 | **Pages:** 7

### Definition

**NYPD Restricted Parking Permit (Misc. 23-N)** — Cardboard, approximately 4" × 10", with serial number, command code, vehicle license plate number, and expiration date.

### Eligibility Requirements

| Requirement |
|-------------|
| Valid NYS driver's license |
| Valid NYS vehicle registration |
| Valid insurance policy |
| Vehicle registered to member, blood/marriage relative, or registered NYS domestic partner |
| Owner's residence matches member's Department address of record |

### Ineligible Members/Vehicles

| Ineligible |
|------------|
| Member assigned Category I vehicle |
| Members at One Police Plaza with garage access (except Category II) |
| Members on Military & Extended Leave Desk or vacation/terminal leave pending retirement |
| Members under suspension |
| Auxiliary Police members |
| Vehicle with **5+ red light camera violations** or **15+ school speed camera violations** within 12 months |

### Application Schedule (By Borough)

| Borough | Member Submission Deadline | ICO Delivery Deadline |
|---------|--------------------------|----------------------|
| Manhattan | Last business day, 1st week of September | 2nd week of September |
| Bronx | Last business day, 2nd week of September | 3rd week of September |
| Staten Island/Brooklyn | Last business day, 3rd week of September | 4th week of September |
| Queens | Last business day, 4th week of September | 1st week of October |

### ICO Background Checks

| System | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| ZFINEST RALL | NYS Vehicle Registration by Plate Number |
| ZFINEST DCID | NYS Driver Inquiry (suspensions, revocations, scofflaw) |
| CPR Employee Inquiry Report | Department personnel records |
| DAS Summons History | Search by license plate |
| NYC Dept of Finance website | Outstanding parking/photo enforcement fines |

### Key Restrictions

- Permit may **NOT be photocopied or reproduced** — subject to disciplinary action
- Permit may NOT be **laminated**
- Valid ONLY for the vehicle whose license plate appears on the face
- **Non-transferable** — exclusive use of assigned member
- If vehicle changes, notify CO within **30 days**; may use substitute vehicle for **max 30 days**

### Parking Still Prohibited (With or Without Permit)

Double parking, fire hydrant/zone, bus stop/lane, sidewalk, crosswalks/driveways, obstructing traffic, no standing zones, snow emergency, no parking/taxi stand, bicycle lane, no permit zone

> **Exam Alert:** Vehicle with **5+ red light camera** or **15+ school speed camera** violations in 12 months = **ineligible**.

> **Exam Alert:** Restricted Parking Permits may NOT be **photocopied, reproduced, or laminated**.

> **Sergeant Focus:** CO has **final authority** to approve or deny all Restricted Parking Permit applications.

## P.G. 219-30: Radiation Detection Pagers — Personally Assigned

**Date Effective:** 11/18/21 | **Last Revision:** 1.0.110 | **Pages:** 3

### Purpose

To provide for control, accountability, and issuance of radiation detection pagers permanently assigned to individual uniformed members.

### Key Differences from Command-Assigned (219-27)

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Assigned by | Counterterrorism Central Resource Division after training completion |
| Assignment | **Permanently** to individual member (not the command) |
| Documentation | PROPERTY TRANSFER RECEIPT (PD521-145) — copy to CO and ICO |
| Serial number | Listed on member's FORCE RECORD |
| Transfer | Commands **NOT authorized** to transfer between members |
| Carry requirement | At **all times** when performing duty |
| Separation | Surrender to desk officer for return to Patrol Borough Counterterrorism Coordinator |

> **Exam Alert:** Personally assigned radiation pagers must be carried at **all times** while performing duty and are listed on the member's **FORCE RECORD**.
