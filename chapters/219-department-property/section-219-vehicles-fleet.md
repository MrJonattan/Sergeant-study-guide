# Section 219 — Vehicles and Fleet Operations

## P.G. 219-01: Department Vehicles — Inspection at Start of Tour

**Date Effective:** 12/31/24 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 78 | **Pages:** 4

### Purpose

To inspect Department vehicles at the start of each tour to ensure safe and proper operation.

### Vehicle Inspection Checklist

| Item | Check |
|------|-------|
| Gasoline | Adequate level |
| Brakes | Functioning properly |
| Tires | Proper inflation, condition |
| E-Z Pass tag | Present, affixed to windshield |
| Mobility tablet | Present, operational |
| License Plate Reader (LPR) | Operational (if equipped) |
| Lights/siren | Functioning |
| Radio | Operational |

### Required Vehicle Equipment (Patrol)

| Equipment |
|-----------|
| 3-foot polycarbonate shield |
| Ballistic window inserts |
| Patrol kit (PPE) |
| Life ring (commands with waterfront) |

### Patrol Supervisor Vehicle — Additional Equipment

| Equipment |
|-----------|
| Rapid Mobilization Plan |
| Crowbar |
| Sledgehammer |
| Crime scene evidence cones |

> **Sergeant Focus:** Patrol supervisor vehicles carry additional emergency/tactical equipment. Ensure these items are present and serviceable each tour.

## P.G. 219-02: Weekly Vehicle Inspections

**Date Effective:** 12/31/24 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 78 | **Pages:** 1

### Purpose

To ensure weekly accountability and condition reporting for all Department vehicles assigned to the command.

### Key Points

- Inspections conducted **each Sunday** after the second platoon has been posted
- Lieutenant platoon commander/patrol supervisor performs the inspection
- Prepare VEHICLE INSPECTION REPORT (PD571-153)
- Record E-Z Pass tag serial numbers on the report

> **Exam Alert:** Weekly inspections occur on **Sunday** after the **second platoon** is posted.

## P.G. 219-08: Tire Chains

**Date Effective:** 05/20/24 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 31 | **Pages:** 1

### Purpose

To provide for the installation and use of tire chains on Department vehicles.

### Key Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Availability period | **November 1 through March 15** |
| Installation threshold | NOT installed until **more than 4 inches** of snow |
| Front-wheel drive restriction | Tire chains are **NOT** installed on front-wheel drive vehicles |

> **Exam Alert:** Tire chains are NOT installed until there is **more than 4 inches of snow** and are NOT used on **front-wheel drive** vehicles.

## P.G. 219-10: Unmarked Vehicle Utilization Record

**Date Effective:** 05/20/24 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 31 | **Pages:** 2

### Purpose

To account for the utilization of unmarked Department vehicles.

### Vehicle Categories

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **General Purpose** | Standard unmarked vehicles for routine operational use |
| **Special Purpose** | Vehicles assigned for specific operational needs |
| **Category I** | Vehicles not required to be stored at Department facility; permitted to be removed from the City on a regular basis; specifically designated by the Police Commissioner |

> **Exam Alert:** **Category I** vehicles are specifically designated by the **Police Commissioner** and may leave the city regularly. Members with Category I vehicles are **ineligible** for Restricted Parking Permits.
