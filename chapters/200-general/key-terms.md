# Key Terms — Section 200: General

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Mission** | The core purpose of the NYPD: enhance the quality of life in NYC by working in partnership with the community |
| **Vision** | The aspirational goal: a safe and fair City through strategic deployment, crime prevention, quality-of-life focus, and community relationships |
| **Values** | Four pledges: protect lives/property, fight crime, maintain integrity, value human life with courtesy and civility |
| **Quality of Life** | A recurring theme across the mission and vision — refers to both crime reduction and addressing community concerns |
| **Community Partnership** | The foundational approach — the NYPD works WITH the community, not just FOR or OVER it |
| **Strategic Deployment** | Allocating resources based on data and need, central to the NYPD's vision |
| **Impartially** | Enforcing the law without bias or favoritism — a core value |
