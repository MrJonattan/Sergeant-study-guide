# Section 329 — Retirement and Resignation

## A.G. 329-06: Discontinuance of Police Service — Retirement or Vested Interest

**Date Effective:** 03/03/26 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 22 | **Pages:** 6

### Purpose

To apply for discontinuance of police service (retirement or vested interest).

### Key Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Terminal Leave** | Leave with pay, computed at Police Pension Fund (PPF), granted to retiring UMOS upon request |
| **LEOSA Certification Card** | Wallet-sized card exempting retired law enforcement officers from local/state prohibitions on carrying concealed firearms; must be accompanied by valid IDENTIFICATION CARD marked "Retired" |

### Terminal Leave Computation

| Service | Leave Granted |
|---------|--------------|
| Per 10 years of service | One calendar month (28, 30, or 31 days depending on month) |
| Beyond 20 years | 3 days per completed year of service |
| Beyond 20 years (partial) | 1 day per completed 4 months of service |

### Terminal Leave NOT Granted When

| Condition |
|----------|
| Lump Sum payment option selected |
| Discontinuing with vested interest |
| Disciplinary charges are pending |
| Time spent on extended leave without pay (unless mandated by law) |

### Prior to Reporting to PPF — Member Duties

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Notify operations coordinator |
| 2 | Email PPF at retirement@nycppf.org for paperwork and appointment |
| 3 | Confer with timekeeper to determine current leave balances |
| 4 | Prepare and forward **PROPERTY RECEIPT - DISCONTINUANCE OF SERVICE (PD520-013)** to CO/designee |
| 5 | Prepare digital Leave of Absence Request(s) to use accrued pre-separation leave |

### Reporting to PPF

| Tier | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| **Tier 2** | Must report at least **30 calendar days** in advance of discontinuance |
| **Tier 3** | May report **without notice** at time of discontinuance |
| All members (terminal leave) | Requested (not required) to report to PPF **10 days** prior to commencement of terminal leave |

### Property Return — Member Delivers

| Property | Deliver To |
|----------|-----------|
| NYC Transit Police Pass, LIRR/Metro-North Police Passes | Desk officer → Employee Resources Section |
| NYPD Restricted Parking Permit, HQ Annex Parking Permit | Integrity control officer/counterpart |
| NYPD Vehicle Identification Plate (Misc. 740) | Chief of Department's Vehicle Identification Unit |
| Ballistic Tactical Helmet, Tactical Retreat Hood | Quartermaster Section, CBRN Unit |
| Body-Worn Camera | ITB Telecommunications Unit |
| Portable radio, laptop/tablet, cellular telephone, MIFI/VPN | ITB Telecommunications Unit |
| Shield and IDENTIFICATION CARD | Shield, ID, and Uniform Services Unit (after PPF conferral) |
| Authorized firearms (if not LEOSA qualified and not applying for handgun license) | Manhattan Property Clerk |
| Helmet and O.C. pepper spray with holster | Firearms and Tactics Section, Police HQ Range |
| Bullet resistant vest | Police Academy, Ballistic Vest Unit (optional — may retain) |

### LEOSA Certification Requirements

| Requirement |
|------------|
| Must have **qualified during regular Department range cycle within past 12 months** |
| Card expires **12 months** from date of last range requalification |
| At least **20 years of service** OR retiring due to **line of duty injury** |
| Separating in **"good standing"** |
| Tier 3: Not issued retired ID or LEOSA card until **30 days** after discontinuance (unless reported to PPF 30 days prior) |

### Terminal Leave Rules

| Rule |
|------|
| Members continue to accrue chart time while exhausting leave balances **except while on terminal leave** |
| No leaves of any kind may be granted after commencement of terminal leave |
| May NOT be placed on sick report unless from **line of duty action while on terminal leave** |
| Tier 2: Must retire no later than midnight of eve of **63rd birthday** |
| Tier 3: Must retire no later than midnight of eve of **62nd birthday** |
| Members on terminal leave are **excluded from excusal limitations** — available only for **emergency situations** |
| Members ordered to work on terminal leave compensated with **cash overtime only** |
| COs should NOT approve programmatic overtime, fill details, or non-emergency duties for these members (except with bureau chief/DC approval) |

### Firearm Retention (Post-1/15/13 Issued Firearms)

| Requirement |
|------------|
| Obtain **NICS check** through Federal Firearms Licensee (FFL) no more than **30 days** prior to separation |
| Deliver completed **ACQUISITION OR DISPOSITION OF FIREARMS BY POLICE OFFICERS - REPORT TO N.Y. STATE POLICE (PD424-150)** to Firearms and Tactics Section prior to separation |

> **Exam Alert:** Tier 2 must report to PPF **30 calendar days** in advance. Terminal leave = 1 month per 10 years + 3 days per year beyond 20. LEOSA card requires qualification within **12 months** and **20 years of service** or line of duty injury.

> **Sergeant Focus:** Know the property return matrix. Tier 2 mandatory retirement age is **63**; Tier 3 is **62**. Members on terminal leave are only available for **emergencies**. No leaves after terminal leave starts.

## A.G. 329-07: Discontinuance of Police Service — Resignation

**Date Effective:** 01/06/26 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 3 | **Pages:** 5

### Purpose

To facilitate the resignation of a uniformed member of the service.

### Definition

**RESIGNATION** — Voluntary separation from the Department other than retirement/vested interest.

### Key Rule — Reinstatement

Resignation **bars reinstatement** EXCEPT if approved by the **Police Commissioner** within **one year** of date of resignation.

### CO Duties

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 2 | Instruct member that resignation bars reinstatement (except Police Commissioner approval within 1 year) |
| 3 | Request member complete RESIGNATION AND EXIT INTERVIEW (PD452-151) |
| 4 | Interview member and record responses |
| 5 | Sign and distribute RESIGNATION AND EXIT INTERVIEW within **5 business days** |
| 6 | Have command clerk prepare PROPERTY RECEIPT - DISCONTINUANCE OF SERVICE (PD520-013) |
| 7 | Sign PROPERTY RECEIPT - DISCONTINUANCE OF SERVICE |

### Desk Officer Duties

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 8 | Make Command Log entry of resignation |
| 9 | Obtain NYC Transit OMNY Police Pass, LIRR/Metro-North passes |
| 10 | Notify **Operations Unit** of resignation — provide 8 items of information |
| 11 | Notify Occupational Safety and Health Section if member indicated feeling unsafe |

### Operations Unit Notifications (Upon Learning of Resignation)

| Notified |
|---------|
| Communications Section (FINEST Message) |
| Internal Affairs Bureau |
| Payroll Section |
| Police Pension Fund |
| Human Resources Division |

### Last Working Day

| Rule |
|------|
| Assign member to **2nd platoon** on last working day |
| If last day falls on Saturday/Sunday → report to PPF on Friday; perform clerical duty in command on Saturday/Sunday |
| Report to PPF on last working day in **civilian clothes** |
| Deliver firearms to **borough Property Clerk's Office ONLY** |

### If Member Resigns Other Than In Person

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 43 | Notify Operations Unit |
| 44 | Notify permanent command |
| 45 | C.O., Personnel Orders Division: mail certified letter acknowledging receipt and acceptance |
| 46 | Attempt personal communication to determine location of property/firearms |
| 49 | Assign **sergeant and clerical member** to open locker and obtain Department property |

### Vacation Accrual Note

| Rule |
|------|
| Members in rank of PO through deputy chief receive entire vacation allotment on January 1 |
| Vacation time only **accrued** while in full pay status |
| Members who resign, are terminated, or take leave without pay — vacation accruals are **prorated** |
| Using vacation in excess of accrual rates prior to resigning → required to **forfeit pay** and/or reimburse Department |
| Resigning in good standing after 1 year of service → compensated for remaining accrued time |

> **Exam Alert:** Resignation bars reinstatement except by **Police Commissioner within 1 year**. Firearms delivered to **borough Property Clerk's Office ONLY**. If member won't report to PPF, CO accepts property. Assign **sergeant** to open locker for absent resignees.

> **Sergeant Focus:** Ensure 8 items of info provided to Operations Unit. Distribute RESIGNATION AND EXIT INTERVIEW within **5 business days**. Members must report to PPF in **civilian clothes**. Vacation is prorated upon resignation.
