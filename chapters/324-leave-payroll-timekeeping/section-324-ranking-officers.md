# Section 324 — Ranking Officers Vacations

## A.G. 324-04: Ranking Officers Vacations

**Date Effective:** 11/18/21 | **Last Revision:** I.O. 99 | **Pages:** 2

### Purpose

To establish vacation selection guidelines specific to ranking officers (sergeant and above), including day entitlements, week types, and mutual exclusion rules.

### Vacation Day Entitlements

| Rank | Annual Days |
|------|-------------|
| **Sergeant and Lieutenant** | **27 vacation days** per year (accrued on January 1st) |
| **Captain through Deputy Chief** | **26 vacation days** per year (accrued on January 1st) |

### Week Types

Ranking officers may select **up to five separate weeks' vacation**, however:
- Only **three weeks** may be **Type "A" weeks**
- Ranking officers should use **entire weeks** when possible
- Ranking officers are also entitled to **two individual vacation days (IVDs)** per year, selected in conjunction with annual vacation selection

### Vacation Selection Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **2% limit** | Only **2% of the unit rank complement** are entitled to use an IVD on any given day |
| **Holiday restriction** | May not select more than one of: New Year's Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Day as an IVD |
| **Mutual exclusion** | Precinct/Unit Operations Coordinators may not be on vacation at the same time as the Commanding Officer |
| **ICO rules** | Precinct Integrity Control Officers (lieutenant or sergeant) select vacation separately from other lieutenants/sergeants; if two ICOs, they may **not** be on vacation at the same time |
| **Transfer/promotion** | Vacation selections are **not automatically retained** when transferred or promoted; may be retained if command efficiency is not impaired |
| **Borough Commander** | Neither Borough Commander nor Borough Executive Officer may select the same vacation pick unless another BEO is available |

### Patrol Borough Grouping

In Patrol Borough and Bureau commands:
- **Inspectors and Deputy Inspectors** are grouped together for vacation selection
- Selection order determined by **seniority in rank** — Inspectors pick first, then Deputy Inspectors

### Supervisor Responsibilities

- Verify **chart assignments** reflect vacation selections
- Ensure **Operations Coordinator** selections don't conflict with C.O.
- For ICO positions, coordinate to ensure **at least one ICO on duty** at all times
- Prepare and maintain **alternate vacation selection plans** consistent with command needs

> **Exam Alert — Sergeant Vacation Entitlement:** Sergeants and lieutenants receive **27 days** — not 26. Captain through deputy chief receive 26. This 27 vs. 26 distinction is frequently tested. Also know the **3 Type A weeks / 5 total weeks** limit.

> **Memory Aid — RANKING VACATION DAYS:** **27** for **Sgt/Lt** (27 = two sevens, "seven-teir"), **26** for **Captain+** (26 = 13+13, "too few"). **3** Type A weeks out of **5** total.
