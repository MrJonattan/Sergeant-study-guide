# Section 324 — Vacation Lost Due to Sick Leave

## A.G. 324-03: Vacation Lost Due to Sick Leave

**Date Effective:** 10/20/25 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 104 | **Pages:** 2

### Purpose

To establish procedures for members who lose vacation time due to being on sick report — ensuring they can reschedule lost vacation while maintaining Department operations.

### Key Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Application timing** | Members apply for vacation lost during the preceding year **without delay, upon return to duty** |
| **Accrued vacation guidelines** | **Do NOT apply** to vacation time lost due to sick leave |
| **Vacation selection** | Members may select alternative dates for vacation lost due to sick leave |
| **Supervisory tracking** | Supervisors must monitor when members are on extended sick leave and ensure timely vacation reselection |
| **Sick report interaction** | When a member is on sick report during a scheduled vacation period, the vacation days are converted to sick leave — not lost permanently |

### Sergeant Responsibilities

- Track members on **extended sick report** who had scheduled vacation
- Ensure members **apply for reselection** immediately upon return to duty
- Verify that **accrued vacation guidelines** (e.g., 12% command limit) are **waived** for sick-leave-recovered vacation
- Document in **Command Diary** and ensure **timekeeper** is notified
- Coordinate with **Operations Coordinator** to fill vacant tour periods

> **Exam Alert — Sick Leave vs. Vacation:** When a member is on sick report during their vacation, those days are **not counted as vacation** — they become sick leave days. The member does not lose the vacation permanently but must reschedule it. This is a common exam scenario.

> **Memory Aid — SICK VACATION INTERACTION:** **Vacation lost → sick report → reschedule without delay → accrued guidelines waived.**
