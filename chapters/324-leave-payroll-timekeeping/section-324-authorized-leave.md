# Section 324 — Authorized Leave

## A.G. 324-05: Authorized Leave

**Date Effective:** 08/11/25 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 70 | **Pages:** 3

### Purpose

To establish procedures for requesting and approving authorized leave of absence, including location tracking, leave without pay, and extended leave requirements.

### Request Procedure

**Member:**
1. Prepare **Digital Leave of Absence Request** via CPR System
2. Submit to commanding officer/supervisory head at least **five days** before leave commences (except emergencies)
3. Complete the **'Location During Absence'** section with country, city/town or province, dates of departure and return
4. If remaining at residence, enter 'residence' with location and dates

**Civilian members** with CityTime access must also prepare a Digital Leave of Absence Request.

### Leave Without Pay

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Accrued leave first** | Members granted extended leave without pay must take all **accrued leave** prior to start of leave of absence (except military leave) |
| **Vacation reduction** | Leave without pay for **30+ consecutive days** reduces authorized vacation by **1/12th** for each 30 consecutive days |
| **One-year return** | Members returning from leave without pay for **one year or more** may not receive unaccrued vacation until **3 months of active duty** |
| **Religious leave** | Leave without pay may be granted to observe religious holidays — no more than **1/6th of each squad** |
| **Extended leave** | Members applying for educational leave, hardship leave, etc., must contact the **Military and Extended Leave Desk** |

### Operations Coordinator Responsibilities

9. Ensure all members submit Digital Leave of Absence Requests **five days in advance** (except emergencies)
10. Access **Leave of Absence - Location During Absence database** when member travels to a foreign country
11. Verify mandatory entries during **monthly self-inspections**
12. Ensure compliance with required entries during monthly self-inspections

### Emergency Excusal Day (E-Day)

When a uniformed member requests an emergency excusal day (E-day) for their next scheduled tour:
- Must receive approval from the **desk officer on duty** at time of request
- May **only request** during the interval between last tour performed and start of next scheduled tour
- **Denial is final** — members may not attempt to get approval from another supervisor; disciplinary action may result
- Desk officer makes entry in **CPR System Roll Call** (rank, name, granted/denied)
- If granted, desk officer ensures **Digital Leave of Absence Request** is prepared

> **Exam Alert:** The 5-day advance submission requirement applies to most leave. E-Day denial by the desk officer is FINAL — this is a common exam question. Also know the 1/6th squad limit for religious leave and the 30-day leave-without-pay = 1/12th vacation reduction formula.

> **See Also:** A.G. 324-01 (Vacation Policy), A.G. 324-06 (Bereavement Leave)

## Compensatory Time Guidelines

Members covered by the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA) use compensatory time in this order:
1. **FLSA time** (Post-April 14, 1986)
2. **Non-FLSA time** (Post-April 14, 1986)
3. **Pre-April 15, 1989 compensatory/exempt time**

Members who have **filed for retirement** may utilize **accrued non-FLSA compensatory time** without first depleting their FLSA balance.
