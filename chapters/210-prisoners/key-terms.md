# Section 210 — Key Terms, Forms, and Critical Numbers

---

## Core Prisoner Definitions

| Term | Definition | Source |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Holdover Prisoner** | A prisoner who has entered the court process, has NOT yet been arraigned, and has been returned to police custody for overnight detention | P.G. 210-01 |
| **Voided Arrest** | Release of a prisoner when reasonable cause exists to believe the prisoner did NOT commit the offense, or that no offense was committed | P.G. 210-13 |
| **Decline Prosecution** | DA exercises discretion not to prosecute a validly arrested prisoner; requires a Decline Prosecution Letter from an ADA | P.G. 210-13 |
| **High Risk Prisoner** | A prisoner assessed as high risk based on: seriousness of offense, refusal to identify, forcible resistance, threats of violence/escape, history of violence, weapons possession, or escape/attempted escape history | P.G. 210-01 |
| **Prisoner Security Guidelines** | All prisoners will be searched on entry to and exit from any police facility, detention facility, DA facility, or court facility; also searched before transport departure and upon arrival | P.G. 210-01 |

## Medical and Arraignment Terms

| Term | Definition | Source |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Mobile Livescan Device (MLD)** | Portable fingerprint scanner that transmits via cellular to DCJS; used exclusively for fingerprinting hospitalized prisoners; requires at least 2 MOS present | P.G. 210-05 |
| **Bedside Arraignment** | Arraignment conducted at the hospital to reduce manpower required to guard hospitalized prisoners | P.G. 210-05 |
| **Prison Ward** | Designated area within a hospital for the treatment and detention of prisoners under guard | P.G. 210-02 |

## Transport and Restraint Terms

| Term | Definition | Source |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Transport Chains** | Restraint system used when transporting more than 2 prisoners; cuffs must be double locked | P.G. 210-01 |
| **Leg Restraints** | Additional restraint applied to legs; MUST be used when prisoner refused fingerprinting and for ALL hospital transports; may NOT be used for punitive purposes; does not replace rear handcuffing | P.G. 210-01 |
| **Rear Cuff** | Standard restraint for ALL prisoners during transport; hands cuffed behind the back | P.G. 210-01 |

## Police Action Terms

| Term | Definition | Source |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Urgent Police Action** | Response to an emergency, active pursuit, or moving surveillance | P.G. 209-30 |
| **Necessary Police Action** | Less than urgent; enhances safety of an operation; NOT solely for the member's convenience | P.G. 209-30 |

## Other Agency Terms

| Term | Definition | Source |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Other Police Agencies** | DOC, NYS DOCCS, Court Officers, Port Authority Police, MTA Police, Amtrak Police, Sea Gate Police, FDNY Fire Marshals, DEP Police, TBTA Police | P.G. 210-03 |

| Medical Treatment of Prisoner | **PD244-150** | Documents medical treatment provided to or requested by a prisoner | P.G. 210-04 |
| Transport Dispatch | **PD171-132** | Records prisoner transport details including destination and escort | P.G. 210-01 |

| OLBS Arrest Worksheet | — | Must include reason for void, property returned, witnesses, authorizing supervisor, and time of release for voided arrests | P.G. 210-13 |
| Expense Report | — | Submitted by the 15th of each month for prisoner meal expenditures | P.G. 210-06 |

## Abbreviations

| Abbreviation | Full Name | Context |
|-------------|-----------|---------|
| **BCS** | Borough Court Section | Coordinates cell space, intake processing, court appearances |
| **DCJS** | Division of Criminal Justice Services | Receives fingerprint transmissions; records sealed for voided arrests |
| **DOC** | Department of Correction (NYC) | Listed as "other police agency" for hospitalized prisoner guarding |
| **DOCCS** | Department of Corrections and Community Supervision (NYS) | State corrections; listed as other police agency; also authorized to interview prisoners |
| **MLD** | Mobile Livescan Device | Portable fingerprint scanner for hospitalized prisoners |
| **MOS** | Member of Service | NYPD personnel |
| **OLBS** | On-Line Booking System | Used to process arrest worksheets including voided arrests |
| **TBTA** | Triborough Bridge and Tunnel Authority | Listed as other police agency |
