# Section 305 — Lost or Damaged Uniform

## A.G. 305-05: Lost or Damaged Uniform

**Date Effective:** 06/24/21 | **Last Revision:** I.O. 63 | **Pages:** 3

### Purpose

To reimburse uniformed members of the service for uniforms or equipment damaged or lost in the performance of police duty, as well as personal clothing or prescription eyeglasses/contact lenses damaged or lost during extraordinary police action (e.g., pursuit, physical altercation, rescues).

### Member Duties

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Prepare report on **Typed Letterhead** addressed to Deputy Commissioner, Management and Budget, within **30 days** from incident |
| 1a | State how/when loss or damage occurred |
| 1b | State date of purchase and cost of article; attach receipt if available |
| 2 | Attach statement of witnesses, if any |
| 3 | Ascertain if article can be repaired; obtain estimated cost for repair OR statement that item is damaged beyond repair |
| 4 | Present damaged article, **unrepaired**, to commanding officer with report and documentation |

### Note on Incapacitated Members

Report must be signed and submitted within **30 days** by member requesting reimbursement. If member is incapacitated, supervisory officer will state so in report, sign, and submit within 30 days.

### Commanding Officer Duties

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 5 | Investigate and endorse report: facts as stated, damage/loss in performance of duty, article inspected, repairable/beyond repair, authorized civilian attire (if applicable), recommendation to approve/disapprove |
| 6 | Forward report to Deputy Commissioner, Management and Budget: **Direct** if cost < $100; **Through channels** if cost ≥ $100 |

### Reimbursement Limits

| Item | Maximum |
|------|---------|
| Watch | $75.00 |
| Prescription eyeglasses/contact lenses | $100.00 |
| Jacket/Coat | $100.00 |
| Suit Jacket | $100.00 |
| Dress | $100.00 |
| Footwear | $75.00 |
| Dress pants | $75.00 |
| Skirt | $50.00 |
| Other pants (jeans, shorts) | $45.00 |
| Dress shirt/blouse | $40.00 |
| Other shirts (sweatshirt, t-shirt) | $20.00 |
| Other | As determined by Audits and Accounts Unit |

### Key Rules

| Rule |
|------|
| Department does NOT reimburse for personal articles (jewelry, rings, bracelets) except **watches** |
| All settlements are **non-transferable** and must be executed within **180 days** of approval |
| Personal clothing claims only for members authorized to perform duty in civilian clothes during **extraordinary police action** |

> **Exam Alert:** Report must be filed within **30 days**. Settlements must be executed within **180 days** of approval. Direct forwarding for costs under **$100**; through channels for $100 or more.

> **Sergeant Focus:** If member is incapacitated, the supervisory officer must sign and submit the report within 30 days. Verify that damaged article is presented unrepaired and that the member was performing police duty (or extraordinary police action for civilian clothing).
