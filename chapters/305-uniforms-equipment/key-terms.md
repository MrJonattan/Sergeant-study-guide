# Section 305 — Key Terms and Definitions

## Firearms Terminology

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Authorized Firearm** | Service pistols and off-duty pistols/revolvers conforming to Firearms and Tactics Section specifications |
| **Service Pistol** | Primary on-duty firearm |
| **Off-Duty Pistol/Revolver** | Authorized secondary firearm; may be carried in addition to service pistol on duty (not visible) |
| **Special Weapons** | Require CO permission before carrying on duty (see A.G. 305-11) |
| **FORCE RECORD (PD406-143)** | Record of all revolvers/pistols owned, permitted modifications |
| **Tritium Night Sights** | Green night sights required on service pistols (optional for pre-9/12/94 members and off-duty pistols) |
| **Firearms Safety Station** | Used when loading/unloading firearms |

## Holster Requirements

| Requirement | Detail |
|------------|--------|
| Retention | Reliable retention device other than friction alone |
| Coverage | Must cover trigger guard, muzzle, and front sight |
| Inside-waistband | Must have reinforced mouth for one-handed re-holstering |
| Prohibited types | "One size fits all," "clip-on," "belly band" |

## Prohibited Modifications

All aftermarket/non-Department parts; polishing that alters firearm; machining; attaching anything (slip-on grips, grip plugs, rail attachments, belt clips); engraving, decals, painting, etching.

## Uniform Reimbursement

| Term | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Eligible items** | Uniforms/equipment damaged/lost in performance of duty; personal clothing/eyeglasses during extraordinary police action |
| **Filing deadline** | 30 days from incident |
| **Settlement deadline** | 180 days from approval |
| **Direct forwarding** | Cost < $100 |
| **Through channels** | Cost ≥ $100 |
| **Personal articles NOT reimbursed** | Jewelry, rings, bracelets (watches excepted at $75 max) |

## Key Timeframes

| Item | Timeframe |
|------|-----------|
| Report for lost/damaged uniform | Within **30 days** |
| Settlement execution | Within **180 days** of approval |
