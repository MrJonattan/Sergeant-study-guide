# Section 305 — Firearms General Regulations

## A.G. 305-07: Firearms General Regulations

**Date Effective:** 11/18/21 | **Last Revision:** I.O. 110 | **Pages:** 3

### Definition

**AUTHORIZED FIREARM** — Service pistols and off-duty pistols/revolvers that conform to specifications and standards set forth by the Firearms and Tactics Section, Police Academy. The Firearms and Tactics Section is custodian of all authorized on-duty and off-duty firearm parameters.

### Member Duties — Carrying Requirements

| Step | Duty |
|------|------|
| 1 | **Safeguard** firearms at all times |
| 2 | Be **armed at all times** when in NYC with service pistol or off-duty pistol/revolver, unless otherwise directed |
| 3 | Carry **only authorized firearms** when on duty |
| 3a | Off-duty pistol/revolver may be carried **in addition to** service pistol while on duty, provided only the service pistol is visible |
| 3b | CO permission required before carrying **special weapons** while performing duty |
| 4 | Ensure service/off-duty firearms are **fully loaded** with Department-issued ammunition when carried |

### When Member May Be Unarmed (Off Duty)

| Circumstance |
|-------------|
| Possession would create unnecessary risk of loss/theft (sporting activities, beach, pool) |
| On **vacation** |
| Engaged in authorized **off-duty employment** |
| Activity where it would be advisable NOT to carry |
| Likelihood that member will be **consuming alcohol** |

### Holster Requirements

| Requirement |
|------------|
| Must affix tightly to belt (or body for shoulder/ankle holster) with reliable retention device other than friction alone |
| Must cover trigger guard, muzzle, and front sight |
| Inside-waistband holster must have **reinforced mouth** for one-handed re-holstering |

### Prohibited Holsters and Carrying Methods

| Prohibited |
|-----------|
| "One size fits all" holsters |
| "Clip-on" holsters |
| "Belly band" holsters |
| Carrying firearms in briefcases, handbags, fanny packs, hip packs, tote bags, knapsacks, paper bags, or similar devices |

### Firearm Storage and Maintenance

| Rule |
|------|
| Do NOT store or leave firearms in **unattended motor vehicle** (except as authorized, e.g., P.G. 221-10) |
| Transport firearms other than authorized service/off-duty firearms **only** en route to/from practice range; must be armed with authorized firearm and other firearms must be **unloaded** |
| All repairs/modifications performed **solely by Firearms and Tactics Section armorers** |
| Record permitted modifications on **FORCE RECORD (PD406-143)** |
| Pass qualification course of fire before carrying modified firearm |
| Green **"Tritium" night sights** required on service pistols (optional for pre-9/12/94 members and off-duty pistols) |
| Record all revolvers and pistols on **FORCE RECORD** |
| Utilize **firearms safety station** when loading/unloading |

### Prohibited Modifications

All aftermarket/non-Department parts are prohibited, including: extended magazine releases, extended slide stop levers, magazines, sights, flared magazine wells. Also prohibited: polishing that alters the firearm, machining any part, attaching anything (slip-on grips, grip plugs, accessory rail attachments, rubber bands, belt clips), and engraving, adding decals, painting, or etching.

### Court Requirements

| Rule |
|------|
| Be **unarmed** when entering Family Court or Supreme Court Matrimonial Parts (unless arresting officer or on official business) |
| Safeguard firearms at location other than the court, OR deliver to desk officer of precinct where court is located |
| If present as arresting officer — report to court officer supervisor and sign Law Enforcement Officer log |

### Foreign Country Travel

| Rule |
|------|
| **Prohibited** from carrying on person, in luggage, or by other conveyance, any firearm into a **foreign country** (unless on official business or personally participating in sporting event requiring firearm) |
| Must comply with all applicable foreign country firearms rules/regulations |
| Failure to comply = disciplinary action and possible criminal penalties/weapon confiscation |

> **Exam Alert:** Members must be armed at all times in NYC unless specific off-duty exceptions apply. Firearms must NEVER be left in an **unattended motor vehicle**. All modifications must be performed by **Firearms and Tactics Section armorers** only.

> **Sergeant Focus:** Verify holster compliance (retention device, trigger guard coverage, no clip-on/belly band). Ensure members have passed qualification before carrying modified firearms. Know the Family Court/Supreme Court unarmed requirement.
