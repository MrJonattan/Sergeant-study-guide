# Section 305 — Uniforms and Equipment

## Source
- **Administrative Guide Section:** 305
- **Source PDFs:** A.G. 305-05, A.G. 305-07
- **Priority:** Low

## Learning Objectives
- Know reimbursement procedures for lost or damaged uniforms and personal clothing
- Understand firearms carrying requirements — when armed/unarmed, holster requirements
- Identify prohibited holster types and carrying methods
- Know firearm modification, storage, and court entry rules

## Sergeant Focus Areas
- **Holster compliance:** Verify retention device, trigger guard coverage; no clip-on/belly band
- **Modification rules:** Only Firearms and Tactics Section armorers; qualification required after modification
- **Reimbursement:** 30-day filing deadline; direct forwarding for < $100, through channels for ≥ $100
- **Unarmed exceptions:** Know the 5 off-duty circumstances allowing member to be unarmed
- **Court entry:** Members unarmed at Family Court/Supreme Court Matrimonial unless arresting officer
