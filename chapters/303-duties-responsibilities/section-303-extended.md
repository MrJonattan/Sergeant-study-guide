# Section 303 — Borough Command Duties & Responsibilities (Extended)

## A.G. 303-06: Borough Administrator

**Date Effective:** 05/27/21 | **Last Revision:** I.O. 37 | **Page:** 1 of 1

### Duties

1. Report to **Borough Executive Officer, Administration**
2. Oversee **Borough Operations Unit**
3. Administer **communications program**
4. Supervise **scheduling of assigned members**
5. Ensure proper functioning of **court/agency appearances control**
6. Supervise and develop programs ensuring all discoverable material (records, evidence, test results, notes, videos, photos) for all arrests are produced and provided to the **District Attorney's office** in a timely manner
7. Supervise **community affairs functions**
8. Safeguard and ensure **Department property** is in good working order

## A.G. 303-07: Borough Special Projects Coordinator

**Date Effective:** 05/01/25 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 27 | **Page:** 1 of 1

### Duties

1. Report to **Borough Executive Officer, Operations**
2. Oversee the following:
   - a. **Borough Crime Analysis** / Patrol Borough Pattern Identification Module (PIM)
   - b. **Quality of life** initiatives
   - c. **Special project implementation**
   - d. **311 program**
3. Ensure a robust **Traffic Management Safety program** is in place
4. Identify **school safety issues**; develop borough-level school safety strategies and establish a quality control and review mechanism to evaluate programs and initiatives
   - a. Coordinate with School Safety Division

## A.G. 303-08: Borough Counterterrorism Coordinator

**Date Effective:** 12/02/22 | **Last Revision:** I.O. 116 | **Page:** 1 of 1

### Duties

1. Maintain liaison with **Borough Executive Officer(s), Operations** and **Patrol Borough Operations Unit**
2. Oversee and coordinate **counterterrorism training, equipment, and preparedness** for Patrol Borough personnel
3. Oversee and coordinate **counterterrorism security planning** for Patrol Borough locations (critical, sensitive, iconic) and special events
4. Assist Patrol Borough Operations Unit in preparation, maintenance, and operational deployment of **Disorder Control Plan, Area Evacuation Plans**, and **Patrol Borough Stand Alone Plan**
5. Assist the Patrol Borough to ensure **mobilization preparedness**
6. Serve as liaison for Patrol Borough with external agencies on counterterrorism matters

---

## A.G. 303-09: Patrol Borough Traffic Safety Coordinator

**Date Effective:** 10/24/25 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 108 | **Page:** 1 of 1

### Duties

1. Monitor traffic-related activities of **Administrative Quality of Life Officers**
2. Review precinct **Collision Prone Location** and **Enforcement Ratio Reports**
3. Forward Collision Prone Location and Enforcement Ratio reports to **Patrol Services Bureau** by the **10th day** of the month
4. Maintain liaison with **Department of Transportation** regarding engineering concerns not resolved at precinct level
5. Conduct on-site meetings with precinct Administrative Quality of Life Officer and DOT representatives to resolve and expedite engineering recommendations

---

## A.G. 303-10: Borough Auxiliary Police Coordinator

**Date Effective:** 05/27/21 | **Last Revision:** I.O. 37 | **Page:** 1 of 1

### Duties

1. Oversee activities and functions of, and provide support for, **command auxiliary police coordinators**
2. Provide assistance in assignment of auxiliary police personnel for **special events** and **details** within the borough and outside the borough if requested by Commanding Officer, Auxiliary Police Section
3. Process requests from command auxiliary police coordinators:
   - a. **Promotion**
   - b. **Recognition awards**
4. Process return of **shields and ID cards** for members leaving or dropped from the Auxiliary Police Program
5. Submit tours of duty for upcoming week to Auxiliary Police
