# Section 217 — City and Agency Involved

## P.G. 217-04: Accidents and Collisions — City Involved

**Date Issued:** 10/18/16 | **Pages:** 2

### Purpose

To assist the City in processing cases in which the City may be a plaintiff or defendant.

### Key Definition

**CITY INVOLVED** — Any reported incident involving personal injury and/or property damage which may involve the City in a civil action.

> **Exam Alert:** "City Involved" is broadly defined — any incident that **may** involve the City, not just confirmed liability situations.

### Procedure

When it is determined that the City may be involved in a civil suit:

#### Uniformed Member of the Service (Steps 1-4)

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Prepare **Police Accident Report (MV104AN)** if collision, and check appropriate boxes under "DUPLICATE COPY REQUIRED FOR" |
| 2 | **OR** prepare **AIDED REPORT** in cases of personal injury where City may be involved |
| 3 | **OR** prepare **ACCIDENT REPORT — CITY INVOLVED (PD301-155)** only in cases where City may be involved and **no personal injury** resulted (property damage only) |
| 4 | Ensure AIDED REPORT contains indication that City is involved and name of City agency |

> **Exam Alert:** Three possible reports depending on circumstances: MV104AN (collision), AIDED REPORT (personal injury, non-collision), or PD301-155 (property damage only, no personal injury).

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 5 | Inform persons involved that a claim against the City must be filed with the **Comptroller of the City of New York** within **ninety (90) days** |
| 6 | Submit reports to desk officer |

> **Exam Alert:** Claims against the City must be filed with the **Comptroller** within **90 days**.

#### Desk Officer (Steps 7-8)

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 7 | Review and sign/approve reports |
| 8 | Forward reports: Police Accident Report in normal manner; ACCIDENT REPORT — CITY INVOLVED per instructions on form |
| 8a | Dispose of buff agency copy if Police Department is the **ONLY** agency involved |
| 8b | Determine City agency involved per the routing table below |

### Agency Routing Table

| Condition | Agency |
|-----------|--------|
| Traffic signs, stanchions, signal lights | Department of Transportation |
| Lampposts | Department of Transportation |
| Parkway, highway, stone wall, curb, fence, guide rail, post | DOT, Bureau of Highways, Legal Department |
| Property damage on Parks Department property; trees on parkland, city streets, or public property | Department of Parks |
| Damaged fire alarm box/post | FDNY, Actions and Claims Unit |
| City hydrants | Department of Environmental Protection **AND** FDNY, Actions and Claims Unit |
| School bus collision | Department of Education, Office of Pupil Transportation |
| Property damage from transit facilities (e.g., falling debris from elevated MTA line) | NYC Transit Law Department |
| Accidents on/near ferry, bridge, tunnel, dock, airport, or seaplane base | Agency concerned |

> **Exam Alert:** City hydrants require notification to **two** agencies: DEP **and** FDNY Actions and Claims.

> **Sergeant Focus:** It is City policy to **pursue claims in its favor**. Members must detail circumstances supporting City claims and obtain witness information. **Full cooperation** with the involved agency or Law Department is **mandatory**.

### Key Forms

| Form | Number |
|------|--------|
| ACCIDENT REPORT — CITY INVOLVED | PD301-155 |
| AIDED REPORT | (Electronic) |
| Police Accident Report | MV104AN |

## P.G. 217-11: Accidents — New York City Housing Authority Involved

**Date Issued:** 04/13/21 | **Pages:** 2

### Purpose

To assist the New York City Housing Authority in processing cases in which NYCHA may be a plaintiff or defendant.

### Key Definition

**N.Y.C.H.A. INVOLVED** — ALL accidents involving serious injury or death, or an aided case which may involve the Housing Authority in legal action.

> **Exam Alert:** NYCHA Involved covers **ALL** accidents with serious injury/death **or** aided cases with potential legal action — broader than just collisions.

### Procedure

#### Uniformed Member of the Service (Steps 1-3)

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Prepare **FIELD REPORT** using FORMS |
| 1a | A legible **handwritten copy** must be completed if electronic access is not available |
| 2 | Ensure FIELD REPORT contains: |
| 2a | **Contributory conditions** (no lights in stairwell, rubbish on stairs, broken sidewalk, icy condition, etc.) **OR** |
| 2b | **Non-contributory conditions** (stairwell well lighted, dry, no obstructions, sidewalk clear, etc.) |
| 3 | Telephone **PSA desk officer** and report all pertinent details |

> **Exam Alert:** The FIELD REPORT must document **either** contributory **or** non-contributory conditions — one or the other must always be stated.

#### Desk Officer (Steps 4-5)

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 4 | Make determination of severity and make notifications: |
| 4a | **Business hours (Mon-Fri):** Notify manager of NYCHA location AND forward copies to **NYCHA Law Department Tort Division, 75 Park Place, 11th Floor, NYC 10007** |
| 4b | **After hours/holidays/weekends:** Desk officer makes notifications on **next business day** |
| 5 | Make Command Log entry of notification, including name of person notified |

> **Exam Alert:** After-hours NYCHA notifications are deferred to the **next business day** — they are NOT made immediately.

### Additional Data

- Notifications for serious injury or death resulting from **criminal action** are **excluded** from this procedure
- Copies of all reports, including ACCIDENT REPORT — CITY INVOLVED if prepared, forwarded to Housing Authority Insurance Division for informational purposes
- NYCHA policy is to **pursue claims in its favor** — full cooperation is **mandatory**

### Key Forms

| Form | Number |
|------|--------|
| FIELD REPORT | 313-1511 |
| ACCIDENT REPORT — CITY INVOLVED | PD301-155 |
