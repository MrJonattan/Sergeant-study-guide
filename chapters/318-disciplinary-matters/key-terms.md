# Section 318 — Key Terms and Definitions

## Intoxication

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Intoxication** | Unfitness for duty due to influence of alcohol, narcotics, or other drug, or circumstances indicating member may have been intoxicated during earlier period related to the incident |

## Key Forms

| Form | Used For |
|------|---------|
| **SUPERVISOR'S FITNESS FOR DUTY REPORT (PD469-150)** | Prepared by both referring supervisor AND CO/XO/duty captain based on observations |
| **NOTIFICATION OF ALLEGATION(S) AND EMPLOYEE'S RIGHTS (PD339-121)** | Provided to member prior to interrogation for review and endorsement |
| **CHARGES AND SPECIFICATIONS (PD468-121)** | Formal disciplinary charges served on member |

## Interrogation Rights

| Right | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **PD339-121** | Must be provided before questioning |
| **Counsel** | Permitted for serious violations or sufficient justification |
| **BWC Review** | Member may view own BWC recording related to incident prior to questioning |
| **Line Organization** | Representative present at all times during interrogation |
| **Reasonable Time** | Captain or above determines what constitutes "reasonable time" for counsel |

## Garrity Warning Elements

| Element |
|---------|
| Official investigation by Police Department |
| Questions specifically directed and narrowly related to official duties |
| All rights under NY State law, State Constitution, US Constitution |
| Right not to be compelled to incriminate yourself |
| Right to legal counsel at each stage |
| Refusal to testify on official duties = departmental charges → possible dismissal |
| Statements CANNOT be used in criminal proceedings |
| Statements CAN be used in departmental charges |

## Key Timeframes

| Item | Timeframe |
|------|-----------|
| Post-charge interrogation completion | At least **10 days** before Department trial |
| Tape provided after charge service | Within **20 days** |
| Tape provided after post-charge interrogation | Within **5 days** |
| Transcript to member | By **1000 hours** on trial date |
