# Section 318-16 / A.G. 318-16 — Orders of Protection Prohibiting Off-Duty Firearms Possession by Uniformed Members of the Service

## Source Reference
- **Procedure:** A.G. 318-16
- **Source PDF:** Public Admin Guide1.pdf (OCR extracted)
- **Last Revision:** R.O. 78 (12/31/24)

## Procedure Summary

This procedure informs uniformed members of the service of the procedures to follow when served with an Order of Protection prohibiting firearms possession **off-duty only**, and the member is to be continued on **full-duty status**.

## Scope

A uniformed member who is served with an Order of Protection prohibiting **off-duty firearms possession only** may continue on full-duty status if there is no basis for placing the member on modified assignment. The member will be required to **safeguard all firearms while off-duty**.

## Purpose

To establish procedures for:
1. Safeguarding firearms and identification when off-duty possession is prohibited
2. Maintaining on-duty firearms authorization
3. Reporting requirements to Employee Resources Section
4. Process for restoring firearms possession when Order expires or is vacated

## When Order of Protection is Served (Off-Duty Firearms Prohibited Only)

### Commanding Officer / Executive Officer / Duty Captain Shall:

1. **Request member complete ACKNOWLEDGEMENT OF RESPONSIBILITIES CONCERNING COURT ORDER LIMITING FIREARMS POSSESSION (PD424-010)**
   - If member **fails or refuses** to complete this form → **place member on modified assignment**

2. **Ensure all firearms owned or possessed by member have been accounted for**
   - Question member concerning firearms ownership and possession
   - Notify desk officer/supervisor, member's permanent command, request check of appropriate records

3. **Designate a location at member's command** where member's service weapon, authorized off-duty weapon (if applicable), 'FULL DUTY' IDENTIFICATION CARD, and shield are to be safeguarded while member is off-duty

4. **Assist in determining if all firearms have been surrendered**
   - Check member's **FORCE RECORD (PD406-143)**
   - Notify **Police Academy, Firearms and Tactics Section**, request check of records regarding member's firearms ownership
   - Arrange to obtain additional firearms as necessary

5. **Have firearms (other than service weapon and authorized off-duty weapon) vouchered for safekeeping** per P.G. 218-23
   - 'Remarks' section of **PROPERTY CLERK INVOICE (PD521-141)** must include: *"Property of a uniformed member of the service - not to be returned without written authorization of Commanding Officer, Employee Resources Section"*
   - Ensure member receives copy of INVOICE

### Desk Officer / Supervisor, Member's Permanent Command Shall:

6. **Notify Internal Affairs Bureau (IAB) Command Center** of Order of Protection and limited firearm possession and **obtain log number**
   - Enter log number in 'IAB Log No.' caption on the ACKNOWLEDGEMENT form

7. **Forward copy of ACKNOWLEDGEMENT form and copy of PROPERTY CLERK INVOICE** to commanding officer/executive officer/duty captain

8. **Prepare report on Typed Letterhead** with related details (IAB log number, PROPERTY CLERK INVOICE number, status of Order of Protection, etc.) and deliver to desk officer

### Member of the Service Shall:

9. **Report to the Employee Resources Section as directed**, with all of the following:
   - 'FULL DUTY' IDENTIFICATION CARD
   - Original copy of ACKNOWLEDGEMENT OF RESPONSIBILITIES CONCERNING COURT ORDER LIMITING FIREARMS POSSESSION
   - Copy of report prepared by commanding officer/executive officer/duty captain
   - Copy of PROPERTY CLERK INVOICE for vouchered firearms, if applicable

10. **Report to the Shield, ID and Uniformed Services Unit as directed** and obtain a **'NO FIREARMS' IDENTIFICATION CARD**

## Guidelines for Safeguarding Designated Items

### Uniformed Member of the Service Shall:

11. **Prior to signing out at end of each tour:**
    - Deliver service weapon, authorized off-duty weapon (if applicable), 'FULL DUTY' IDENTIFICATION CARD, and shield to desk officer/supervisor
    - Retrieve **'NO FIREARMS' IDENTIFICATION CARD** from desk officer/supervisor

12. **When reporting for duty each day:**
    - Retrieve service weapon, authorized off-duty weapon (if applicable), 'FULL DUTY' IDENTIFICATION CARD, and shield from desk officer/supervisor
    - Deliver **'NO FIREARMS' IDENTIFICATION CARD** to desk officer/supervisor

### Desk Officer / Supervisor Shall:

13. **Make Command Log entry** when safeguarding or returning member's service weapon, authorized off-duty weapon (if applicable), 'FULL DUTY' or 'NO FIREARMS' IDENTIFICATION CARD, and shield
    - Safeguard items in designated location

14. **Telephone Central Records Division, Identification Unit**

15. **Conduct Order of Protection inquiry via FINEST system**

## When Order of Protection is Expired or Vacated

### Uniformed Member of the Service Shall:

16. **Notify commanding officer**

### Commanding Officer Shall:

17. **Verify that Order of Protection is expired or has been vacated** and that there are no active Orders of Protection against member concerned:
    - Contact court of issuance
    - Telephone Central Records Division, Identification Unit
    - Conduct Order of Protection inquiry via FINEST system

18. **Prepare report on Typed Letterhead to Commanding Officer, Employee Resources Section** with status of Order of Protection
    - Forward copy to Commanding Officer, Performance Analysis Unit

19. **Instruct member NOT to carry any firearms while off-duty** until authorization is received from Commanding Officer, Employee Resources Section

20. **Direct member to report to the Employee Resources Section**

### Member of the Service Shall:

21. **Report to Employee Resources Section as directed**, with all of the following:
    - 'NO FIREARMS' and 'FULL DUTY' IDENTIFICATION CARDS
    - Copy of expired or vacated Order of Protection
    - Copy of report prepared by commanding officer/executive officer/duty captain

> **Sergeant Focus:** Member will NOT be permitted to carry any firearms while off-duty until commanding officer receives authorization from Commanding Officer, Employee Resources Section.

22. **Report to Property Clerk Office** with:
    - 'FULL DUTY' IDENTIFICATION CARD
    - Copy of PROPERTY CLERK INVOICE
    - Original report from Commanding Officer, Employee Resources Section, authorizing return of all firearms

## Key Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **'FULL DUTY' IDENTIFICATION CARD** | Identification card issued to uniformed members authorized to carry firearms on and off duty |
| **'NO FIREARMS' IDENTIFICATION CARD** | Identification card issued when off-duty firearms possession is prohibited; exchanged daily for service weapon |
| **Order of Protection** | Court order prohibiting certain conduct toward protected person(s); may include firearms restrictions |
| **Modified Assignment** | Duty status change removing member from full policing duties (e.g., no firearms, administrative duties) |
