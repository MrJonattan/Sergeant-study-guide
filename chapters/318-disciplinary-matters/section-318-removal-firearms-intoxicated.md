# Section 318 — Removal of Firearms from Intoxicated UMOS

## A.G. 318-10: Removal of Firearms from Intoxicated Uniformed Member of the Service

**Date Effective:** 09/06/22 | **Last Revision:** I.O. 93 | **Pages:** 2

### Purpose

To determine if an on/off duty uniformed member of the service is unfit for duty due to intoxication.

### Definition

**INTOXICATION** — Unfitness for duty due to the influence of alcohol, narcotics, or other drug, or under circumstances in which surrounding events of a timely nature indicate the member may have been intoxicated during an earlier period directly related to the incident in question.

### Supervisory Member Duties

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Direct that member remain at Department facility or other location pending arrival of CO/duty captain |
| 2 | Prepare, **immediately**, SUPERVISOR'S FITNESS FOR DUTY REPORT (PD469-150) based upon observations |
| 3 | Notify CO/XO/duty captain to respond |

### CO/XO/Duty Captain Duties

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 4 | Prepare, **immediately**, SUPERVISOR'S FITNESS FOR DUTY REPORT based upon observations |
| 5 | Conduct investigation to determine if member is unfit for duty due to intoxication at time of alleged misconduct |
| 6 | **Remove firearms** when determined that member is intoxicated (see A.G. 318-13, "Removal and Restoration of Firearms") |
| 7 | Place member on **modified assignment** or **suspend from duty**, as appropriate |
| 8 | Advise member of availability of Counseling Service Programs |
| 9 | Have supervisory officer contact **Counseling Services Unit DIRECT** during normal business hours; at other times, request conferral through Sick Desk supervisor |
| 10 | Notify **Internal Affairs Bureau Command Center** and obtain a log number |

### Determination Standard

| Criteria |
|----------|
| Apply **common sense standards** to determine fitness for duty |
| Examine **totality of the circumstances** |
| Consider all credible relevant information |
| Consider: all SUPERVISOR'S FITNESS FOR DUTY REPORTS, witness statements (civilian and MOS), available scientific evidence (Breathalyzer, blood test, etc.) |
| Factor in **time elapsed** since alleged misconduct or first supervisory observation |

### Reporting Requirements

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 11 | Prepare **five copies** of all completed SUPERVISOR'S FITNESS FOR DUTY REPORTS (CO/XO/duty captain's and referring supervisor's) |
| 12 | Prepare **eight copies** of report on Typed Letterhead detailing observations/circumstances, with copies of all FITNESS FOR DUTY REPORTS, forward to: |

| First Deputy Commissioner | Through channels |
| Chief of Department | **DIRECT** |
| Chief of Personnel | **DIRECT** |
| Chief of Internal Affairs | **DIRECT** |
| Deputy Commissioner, Trials | **DIRECT** |
| Department Advocate's Office | **DIRECT** |

### Mandatory Counseling Referral

Prior to final adjudication of a disciplinary matter, in **all misconduct cases in which the use of alcohol is indicated**:
- Conferral with **Employee Assistance Unit** must be made
- Assessment and/or referral by **Counseling Services Unit** must be conducted
- Department Advocate's Office will ensure these steps are taken

### Note on Counseling Services

A supervisory officer is **mandated** in all cases to contact the Counseling Services Unit on behalf of a member who is placed on modified assignment, suspended, or has firearms removed due to being unfit for duty. Counseling Service Program services are **NOT available** for illegal drug use/abuse problems.

> **Exam Alert:** Supervisory member prepares PD469-150 **immediately**. CO/XO/Duty Captain also prepares their own PD469-150. Firearms are removed per A.G. 318-13. **Eight copies** of the Typed Letterhead report are distributed to eight recipients.

> **Sergeant Focus:** The first supervisor on scene must (1) direct member to remain, (2) prepare PD469-150 immediately, (3) notify CO/XO/duty captain. Supervisory officer is **mandated** to contact Counseling Services Unit. IAB Command Center must be notified for log number.
