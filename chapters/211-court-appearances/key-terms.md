# Key Terms — Section 211: Court & Agency Appearances

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **CPR System** | Centralized Personnel Resource System — monitors and controls all court and agency appearances |
| **Short Date Notification** | Notification to appear the next day (or Friday for Monday) — time-sensitive, triggers email notifications to member, roll call, operations coordinator, and integrity control officer |
| **Telephone Alert** | System to prevent unnecessary court appearances; member assigned to 2nd platoon, no meal before 1300 hours; if not called by 1300, alert NOT activated |
| **Appearance Control Unit (ACU)** | Processes all court appearance notifications, mediates conflicts between appearances, monitors member attendance |
| **Officer Time Slip** | Computerized slip from TVB showing hearing disposition; replaces Court Attendance Record (PD468-141) for TVB appearances |
| **FOIL** | Freedom of Information Law — NYS Public Officers Law Sections 84-89; all government records presumed available for public inspection; 5 business day response required |
| **Records Access Officer** | Supervisor of the FOIL Unit within the Legal Bureau's Subpoena Litigation Unit |
| **Command Liaison Officer** | Every commanding officer — designated official liaison for tracking records requests from the Subpoena Litigation Unit |
| **Subpoena Litigation Unit** | Legal Bureau sub-unit located at One Police Plaza, Room 110C; processes all subpoenas and FOIL requests |
| **4th Platoon Rule** | Members on 4th platoon (1800x0200) with next-day court are reassigned to 3rd platoon (1600x2400); members not notified in advance are excused at 2400 (no OT for 2400-0200) |
| **5 Business Days** | (1) Time before testimony to notify Police Commissioner of governmental/private organization appearances; (2) FOIL response deadline |
| **5 Days Maximum** | Temporary DA assignment limit without CO endorsement to Chief of Department |
| **1300 Hours** | Telephone alert cutoff — if member on alert is not called to court by 1300, the alert is NOT activated that day |
| **25 Cents Per Page** | FOIL photocopy fee |
| **30 Days** | Time limit to appeal a FOIL denial to the Deputy Commissioner, Legal Matters |
| **0700-2300 Mon-Fri** | Hours to contact a Department attorney for legal advice; outside these hours, contact CO/duty captain |
| **Brady Obligation** | Prosecution's duty to disclose exculpatory evidence; NYPD possession of records = prosecutor possession; failure to produce = potential conviction reversal |
| **Court Attendance Record (PD468-141)** | Standard court attendance form — notably NOT used for TVB appearances (Officer Time Slip used instead) |
| **Fee Receipt (PD122-017)** | Issued per P.G. 212-79 for out-of-NYC subpoenas |
| **Subpoena Record Book** | Command-level log for tracking all subpoenas and DA/Corporation Counsel requests |
| **Typed Letterhead** | Required format for written notifications to the Police Commissioner, reports to Subpoena Litigation Unit, and Federal criminal subpoena reports |
