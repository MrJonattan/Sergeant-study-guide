# Section 211-09 — Appearances Before Governmental Agencies or Private Organizations

## Purpose

To notify the Police Commissioner when a member intends to testify or make an official statement before a governmental or private organization about Department operations or policies.

---

## Scope

This procedure applies to:

- **Official capacity** testimony
- **Character/opinion/expert** testimony
- Appearances that are **volunteered, invited, or subpoenaed**

---

## Does NOT Apply To

> **Exam Alert:** Know which appearances are excluded from this procedure.

| Excluded Appearances | Reason |
|---------------------|--------|
| Criminal/civil courts | Normal course of duty |
| Grand Jury | Normal course of duty |
| Corporation Counsel | Normal course of duty |
| Trial Room | Normal course of duty |
| DOCCS hearings | Normal course of duty |
| Traffic Violations Bureau (TVB) | Normal course of duty |
| Parking Violations Bureau (PVB) | Normal course of duty |
| OATH | Normal course of duty |
| Non-official capacity speech that is not confidential | Private speech |

---

## Written Request Requirements

Written request prepared in **TRIPLICATE** on **Typed Letterhead** addressed to the **Police Commissioner**.

### Required Information

- Rank, Name, Tax Registry Number, Command
- Agency name and address
- Date and location of appearance
- Type of proceeding
- Date of receipt of request/subpoena
- For **character testimony**: include name, rank, TRN, and command of the person concerned

---

## Forwarding Timeline and Distribution

> **Exam Alert:** The 5 business day rule and triplicate distribution are high-priority exam targets.

Forward **no later than 5 BUSINESS DAYS** before testimony:

| Copy | Routing |
|------|---------|
| **Original** | Police Commissioner — **DIRECT** |
| **Copy** | Police Commissioner — **THROUGH CHANNELS** |
| **Copy** | Deputy Commissioner, Public Information — **DIRECT** |

> **Exam Alert:** Note the difference: one copy goes DIRECT to the PC, one goes THROUGH CHANNELS. A third copy goes DIRECT to DCPI.

---

## Voluntary vs. Subpoenaed Appearances

| Type | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| **Voluntary** | Requires **PRIOR WRITTEN APPROVAL** from Police Commissioner |
| **Subpoenaed** | Member is **required to appear by law**, but written notification to PC is **still required** |

> **Exam Alert:** Even subpoenaed appearances require written notification to the PC. The difference is that voluntary appearances need PRIOR APPROVAL, while subpoenaed appearances need notification (the member must still appear regardless).

---

## Unable to Meet 5-Day Requirement

If unable to meet the 5 business day requirement:

- **Immediately** notify the **Office of the Police Commissioner** or **Legal Bureau** by **telephone**

---

## Conflict with Department Attorney Advice

If a conflict arises regarding testimony:

- Member **MUST FOLLOW** Department attorney advice

---

## Non-Official Capacity Appearances

When speaking in a non-official capacity:

| Rule | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| Statement | Must **clearly state** speaking on own behalf, **not** the Department's |
| Timing | Must be during **OFF DUTY** time |

---

## NY State Attorney General's Law Enforcement Misconduct Investigative Office

> **Exam Alert:** This is a newer provision with strict compliance requirements.

Members **must**:

- Fully cooperate
- Answer all questions **fully and truthfully**
- Honor all requests for documents and evidence

**Failure to comply = disciplinary action**

> **Sergeant Focus:** Supervisors should ensure members understand the mandatory nature of cooperation with the AG's Misconduct Investigative Office. There is no discretion here — full cooperation is required.
