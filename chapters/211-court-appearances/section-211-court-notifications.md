# Section 211-05 & 211-06 — Court Appearance Processing & Telephone Alert System

## P.G. 211-05 — Processing Notifications to Appear at Court and Other Government Agencies

### Purpose

To effectively monitor and control appearances by members of the service at various courts, CCRB, other governmental agencies, and DA assignments via the **Centralized Personnel Resource (CPR) System**.

---

### Short Date Notification

> **Exam Alert:** A **Short Date Notification** is a notification for a member to appear the **next day**, or a notification delivered on **Friday for a Monday** appearance. This is a time-sensitive notification that triggers immediate action.

When a short date notification is received, the CPR System sends email notifications to:

- The member
- Roll call personnel
- Operations coordinator
- Integrity control officer

---

### Roll Call Clerk Responsibilities

> **Sergeant Focus:** The roll call clerk **shall** access the CPR System **3 times daily**:

| Access Time | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **1st access** | Beginning of business day |
| **2nd access** | Midpoint of roll call office hours |
| **3rd access** | 30 minutes before close of roll call office hours |

---

### Acceptable Cancellation Reasons

A cancellation is accepted **only** if the member is:

| Reason | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| Sick | **Only after conferral with District Surgeon** |
| Annual vacation selection | Must be pre-approved |
| Military leave | Verified status |
| Bereavement leave | Verified status |
| Terminal leave | Verified status |

> **Exam Alert:** A voicemail, email, or message left attempting to cancel is **NOT sufficient**. The requesting agency must **EXPLICITLY CONCUR** with the cancellation. If the agency does not concur, the member **MUST** appear as scheduled.

---

### Scheduling Conflicts and RDO Appearances

- **Operations coordinator** contacts the DA office when a member is scheduled on an RDO to try to reschedule
- **Conflicting court appearances:** Member notifies desk officer/supervisor/roll call, who **immediately** notifies the **Appearance Control Unit (ACU)**
- ACU **mediates all conflicts**

---

### Short Date Notification — Desk Officer Duties

> **Sergeant Focus:** The desk officer shall:

1. Monitor CPR "Court Appearances" tile
2. Inspect the Telephone Record
3. Attempt to notify the member by phone if off duty
4. Document all notification attempts

---

### DA Field and Temporary Assignments

| Assignment Type | Requirement |
|----------------|-------------|
| DA Field Assignment | Member needs **prior CO permission** if starting at location other than command/DA/court |
| Temporary DA Assignment | **Not to exceed 5 days** |
| Extension (6-30 days) | Forwarded to CO of member for endorsement to **Chief of Department** |
| Over 30 days | Processed per **A.G. 329-18** |

> **Exam Alert:** The 5-day limit for temporary DA assignments is a frequently tested number.

---

### 4th Platoon Rule

> **Exam Alert:** This is a high-priority exam topic.

Members **normally assigned** to the 4th platoon (**1800x0200**) who are subpoenaed or directed to appear the next day (2nd platoon) **shall be reassigned to the 3rd platoon (1600x2400)**.

| Scenario | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Member notified in advance | Reassigned to 3rd platoon (1600x2400) |
| Member NOT notified in advance (already on 4th platoon) | Excused at **2400 hours** |
| Overtime for 2400-0200 gap | **NO overtime** is authorized for the 2400-0200 period |

---

## P.G. 211-06 — Criminal/Civil Court Telephone Alert System

### Purpose

To **prevent unnecessary court appearances**.

---

### Key Definition

> **Exam Alert:** For purposes of this procedure, **Civil Court** means Civil Court **and** Supreme Court — Civil Term.

---

### Criminal Court Telephone Alert

> **Sergeant Focus:** The roll call clerk shall:

1. Verify member is scheduled for duty
2. Verify member is not on vacation or leave
3. Verify no conflicting appearances exist
4. Make entry in **Command Diary**
5. Assign member to **2nd platoon**

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Meal period | **NO meal period before 1300 hours** |
| If not directed to court by 1300 | Alert is **NOT activated** that day |

> **Exam Alert:** The **1300 hours** cutoff is critical. If a member on telephone alert is not directed to court by 1300, the alert is NOT activated for that day.

---

### Civil Court Telephone Alert

- **Desk officer** enters attorney name, address, and phone number in the **Command Log**
- Roll call notifies the **Appearance Control Unit of the borough where the court is located** (not the member's borough)

> **Exam Alert:** The ACU notification goes to the **court's borough**, not the member's assigned borough.

---

### Member Becomes Unavailable While on Alert

If a member on alert becomes unavailable (sick, new arrest, conflicting court appearance):

- Roll call/desk officer notifies **ACU by telephone**

---

### ACU Supervisor Written Notification to CO

> **Sergeant Focus:** The ACU supervisor shall notify the CO **in writing** when:

| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Member **fails to appear** when called | Written notification to CO |
| Member **appears in court without being called** | Written notification to CO |

> **Exam Alert:** Both failure to appear AND unauthorized appearance trigger written notification. This is a common exam distinction.
