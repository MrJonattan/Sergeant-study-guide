# Section 320 — Key Terms & Critical Numbers

## Definitions

| Term | Definition | Procedure |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| Exchange of Tour | Voluntary performance of another MOS's tour; also known as a "mutual" | 320-01 |
| FLSA Cycle | 28-day Fair Labor Standards Act work cycle | 320-01 |
| Personal Folder | 11" x 14" folder inscribed with name, tax registry, shield, date of appointment | 320-03 |
| Time Record Folder (TRF) | Separate folder for overtime reports — NOT in personal folder | 320-03 |

## Critical Numbers

| Number | What It Means | Procedure |
|--------|--------------|-----------|
| 5 days | Minimum advance submission for Exchange of Tour Request | 320-01 |
| 7 days | Minimum advance notice for off-duty correctional facility visit | 320-02 |
| 24 hours | Department can reschedule any same-rank MOS without this notice if mutual fails | 320-01 |
| 28 days | FLSA cycle — mutuals must be within the same cycle | 320-01 |
| 6 months | Reports older than this are purged from personal folder | 320-03 |
| Jan 1 / Jul 1 | Dates when old reports are purged from personal folders | 320-03 |

## Exchange of Tour Requirements (320-01)

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Rank | Same rank, below captain |
| Assignment | Same command |
| Qualification | Must be qualified to perform each other's duties |
| Timing | Within same 28-day FLSA cycle |
| Consecutive tours | NOT permitted (no 3rd platoon followed by 1st platoon) |
| Sick reporting | Must report Regular Sick (not Administrative Sick); requires Dept surgeon visit |
| Overtime | No FLSA or contractual overtime generated |
| Form | PD433-160 (Exchange of Tour Request) |

## Correctional Facility Visit Requirements (320-02)

| Step | Who | Action |
|------|-----|--------|
| 1 | MOS | Prepare typed letterhead with 6 required data points, submit 7 days in advance |
| 2 | CO | Notify IAB, Command Center |
| 3 | CO | Approve/disapprove |
| 4 | CO | Notify member of determination |
| 5 | CO | Forward copy to Deputy Commissioner, Internal Affairs; file in personal folder |

## Personal Folder — Reports Purged on Jan 1 and Jul 1

1. Officer Profile Report (if > 6 months old)
2. FINEST messages re: sick reports (if > 6 months old)
3. Sick Report Return (if > 6 months old)

> Purged reports are filed and safeguarded until an Operations Order for destruction is received.
