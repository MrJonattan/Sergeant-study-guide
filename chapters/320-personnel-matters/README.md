# Section 320 — Personnel Matters — General

## Source
- **Administrative Guide Section:** 320
- **Source PDF:** Public Admin Guide2.pdf
- **Priority:** Medium

## Overview

Section 320 of the Administrative Guide covers general personnel matters including tour exchanges ("mutuals"), monitoring off-duty visits to correctional facilities, and maintenance/transfer of command-level personal records. These procedures contain specific numeric thresholds and timeframes that are frequently tested on the sergeant exam.

## Priority Topics for Sergeant Exam

1. **Exchange of Tours (320-01)** — 28-day FLSA cycle, same rank/command/below captain, no consecutive tours, 5-day advance submission, PD433-160, Regular Sick only
2. **Correctional Facility Visits (320-02)** — 7-day advance notice, separate notification per visit, IAB notification, copy to DC Internal Affairs
3. **Personal Records (320-03)** — Jan 1/Jul 1 purge dates, 6-month threshold, sealed "Confidential" envelope for transfers, NYS driver's license check

## Study Tips

- The exchange of tours procedure is packed with testable restrictions — memorize: **same rank, same command, below captain, 28-day FLSA cycle, no consecutive tours, 5 days advance, Regular Sick only**
- Know that **PD433-160** is the Exchange of Tour Request form
- The **7-day** advance notice for correctional facility visits and **5-day** advance for tour exchanges are both common exam questions
- For personal records: the **January 1 and July 1** purge dates and **6-month** threshold are classic number questions
- Remember: overtime records go in the **Time Record Folder (TRF)**, NOT the personal folder
