# Section 332 — Employee Rights & Responsibilities

## Source
- **Patrol Guide Section:** 332
- **Source PDF:** public-pguide4.pdf
- **Priority:** High

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the rights afforded to members of the service under department policy and labor agreements
- Know the procedures for bill of rights notifications during investigations and interrogations
- Identify the protections available under the collective bargaining agreement and civil service law
- Recognize the balance between employee rights and the department's need to maintain discipline
