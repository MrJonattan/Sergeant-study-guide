# Section 319 — Review Questions

## Multiple Choice

**1. A civilian member must report an on-duty injury no later than:**

- A) 24 hours after the incident
- B) 3 days after the incident
- C) 7 days after the incident
- D) 30 days after the incident

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**C) 7 days after the incident**

The civilian member must report injury to supervisor at time of incident, and the injury must be reported no later than 7 days after the incident. (A.G. 319-08)

</details>

---

**2. The investigating supervisor must notify the Civilian Employee Services Unit and forward copies within:**

- A) Immediately by telephone; copies within 12 hours
- B) Immediately by telephone during business hours; copies within 24 hours
- C) By end of tour; copies within 48 hours
- D) Within 24 hours; copies within 72 hours

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**B) Immediately by telephone during business hours; copies within 24 hours**

The supervisor must notify the Civilian Employee Services Unit immediately by telephone during business hours (or at 0900 following business day) and forward copies within 24 hours of notification. (A.G. 319-08)

</details>

---

**3. Who is empowered to grant leave with pay for the first week's absence of a civilian member covered by Workers Compensation?**

- A) The commanding officer
- B) The desk officer
- C) The Chief of Personnel
- D) The Deputy Commissioner, Management and Budget

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**C) The Chief of Personnel**

The Chief of Personnel is empowered to grant leave with pay for the first week's absence of a member covered by Workers Compensation who is physically disabled in the performance of official duties. (A.G. 319-08)

</details>

---

**4. How many forms must the investigating supervisor ensure are prepared when a civilian member is injured on duty?**

- A) 3
- B) 4
- C) 5
- D) 6

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**C) 5**

Five forms: Employee Statement Form (FISA WCS-110), Supervisor's Report of Injury (FISA WCS-100), Witness Statement Form (FISA WCS-120), Employee Option Form (DP2002), and Time Usage 49. (A.G. 319-08)

</details>
