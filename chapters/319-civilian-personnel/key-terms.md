# Section 319 — Key Terms and Definitions

## Forms

| Form | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Employee Statement Form (FISA WCS-110)** | Civilian member's statement of injury |
| **Supervisor's/Agency Form - Report of Injury (FISA WCS-100)** | Supervisor's report of civilian injury |
| **Witness Statement Form (FISA WCS-120)** | Witness account of civilian injury |
| **Employee Option Form (DP2002)** | Civilian member's option selection |
| **Time Usage 49** | Time Used Due to Job Injury - Workers Compensation |

## Key Timeframes

| Item | Timeframe |
|------|-----------|
| Civilian member reports injury | No later than **7 days** after incident |
| Supervisor notifies Civilian Employee Services Unit | **Immediately** (business hours) or **0900** following business day |
| Forward copies to Civilian Employee Services Unit | Within **24 hours** of notification |

## Key Authority

| Authority | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| **Chief of Personnel** | Empowered to grant leave with pay for first week's absence (Workers Compensation, disabled in line of duty) |
