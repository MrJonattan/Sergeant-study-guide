# Section 319 — Civilian Member Injury

## A.G. 319-08: Civilian Member - Injury

**Date Effective:** 11/18/21 | **Last Revision:** I.O. 106 | **Pages:** 1

### Purpose

To report injury of a civilian member while on duty.

### Civilian Member Duties

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Report injury to supervisor at time of incident |
| 1a | Injury must be reported **no later than 7 days** after incident |

### Investigating Supervisor Duties

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 2 | Investigate circumstances and ensure preparation of: |
| 2a | Employee Statement Form (**FISA Form WCS-110**) |
| 2b | Supervisor's/Agency Form - "Report of Injury" (**FISA Form WCS-100**) |
| 2c | Witness Statement Form (**FISA Form WCS-120**) |
| 2d | Employee Option Form (**DP2002**) |
| 2e | Time Usage 49 (Time Used Due to Job Injury - Workers Compensation) |
| 3 | Notify **Civilian Employee Services Unit** immediately by telephone during business hours, or at **0900 hours** following business day |
| 3a | Forward copies to Civilian Employee Services Unit within **24 hours** of notification |

### Key Rules

| Rule |
|------|
| Employee Notice of Injury and Employee Option Form obtained from Civilian Employee Services Unit |
| Chief of Personnel is empowered to grant **leave with pay** for first week's absence of a member covered by Workers Compensation who is physically disabled in performance of official duties |

> **Exam Alert:** Civilian member must report injury within **7 days**. Supervisor must notify Civilian Employee Services Unit immediately (or 0900 following business day) and forward copies within **24 hours**.

> **Memory Aid — FIVE FORMS FOR CIVILIAN INJURY:** **WCS-110** (Employee Statement), **WCS-100** (Supervisor Report), **WCS-120** (Witness Statement), **DP2002** (Employee Option), **Time Usage 49** (Workers Comp time tracking). Think: **"S-T-A-W-T"** — **S**upervisor **T**he **A**gency **W**orker's **T**ime forms.

> **Exam Alert — 5 Forms Must ALL Be Prepared:** The investigating supervisor must ensure preparation of ALL five forms. A question may ask which forms are required for a civilian on-duty injury — the answer includes all five. Missing any form is a procedural deficiency.

> **See Also:** A.G. 319-01 through 319-07 — Other civilian personnel procedures

> **Sergeant Focus:** Know the 5 forms required: WCS-110 (Employee Statement), WCS-100 (Supervisor Report of Injury), WCS-120 (Witness Statement), DP2002 (Employee Option), and Time Usage 49. Chief of Personnel grants leave with pay for first week.
