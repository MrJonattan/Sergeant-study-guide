# Section 203-17 / A.G. 304-23 — Fund Raising Events

## Source Reference
- **Procedure:** A.G. 304-23 (cross-referenced as P.G. 203-17)
- **Source PDF:** Public Admin Guide1.pdf
- **Last Revision:** I.O.116 (12/02/22)

## Procedure Summary

This procedure provides specific information relative to fund raising activities affecting the Department or any person connected with the Department, and the posting of advertisements involving such events by members of the service.

## Pre-Event Requirements

**Prior to the fund raising event or posting of advertisements**, the following procedure must be completed:

### Commanding Officer, Member Concerned:

**Shall** prepare a request on **Typed Letterhead**, addressed to **Chief of Internal Affairs** (through channels), containing the following **EIGHT required elements**:

| # | Required Element |
|---|------------------|
| a | **Date, time, and location** of fund-raiser; identities of organizers; recipient of proceeds after expenses |
| b | **Results of inquiries** to Intelligence Division and Detective Bureau relative to premises and principals involved; State Liquor Authority when appropriate |
| c | **Results of check** of respective precinct's Integrity Monitoring File |
| d | **Specific manner** by which funds will be raised, including use of online fund raising sites (e.g., GoFundMe, Kickstarter, etc.); method of compensation to premises principals; assurance that **full retail price** will be paid for all food, beverages, and ancillary services (music, entertainment, etc.) |
| e | **Statement** indicating that **no illegal gambling, raffles, or 50-50 drawings** will take place at the event |
| f | **Statement and documentation** indicating a **specific accounting of funds** collected and disbursed will be forwarded **within 30 days** after the fund-raiser to Chief of Internal Affairs (copies of checks, bank deposit slips, or proof of payment for outstanding bills, etc.) |
| g | **Statement** indicating that no conflict with Department procedures and/or guidelines was found to exist relative to fundraiser |
| h | **Concluding remark** recommending approval of the request |

### Forward Request:

**Shall** forward completed request through channels to **Chief of Internal Affairs**.

## Chief of Internal Affairs Bureau:

**Shall:**
- Advise commanding officer submitting request of the **IAB number** assigned to the request
- Advise whether request was **approved or disapproved** by Chief of Internal Affairs
- Return request, if approved, to bureau chief/counterpart concerned for appropriate follow-up and event monitoring

> **Sergeant Focus:** Request must be submitted **prior to** the event or posting advertisements. IAB will monitor the event.

> **Exam Alert:** Accounting of funds must be forwarded within **30 days** after the fund-raiser.

> **Memory Aid — FUND RAISING:**
> - **F**orward to Chief of IAB (Typed Letterhead, through channels)
> - **U**se of online sites disclosed (GoFundMe, Kickstarter)
> - **N**o illegal gambling/raffles/50-50 (must state in writing)
> - **D**ate, time, location, organizers, proceeds recipient
> - **R**etail price paid for food/beverages/services
> - **A**ccounting within 30 days (checks, deposits, bills)
> - **I**ntelligence/Detective Bureau checks completed
> - **N**o conflict with Department procedures (must state)
> - **G**etting IAB number (assigned to request)
