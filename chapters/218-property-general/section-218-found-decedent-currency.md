# Section 218 — Found Property, Decedent's Property, and Currency

## P.G. 218-26: Processing Found Property

**Date Effective:** 05/20/24 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 31 | **Pages:** 2

### Purpose

To record and process found property.

### Scope

Found property valued at **$10 or more** must be reported to and deposited with police.

### Special Locations

| Location | Rule |
|----------|------|
| NYC Subway System | Deliver to nearest NYC Transit Station Agent for forwarding to NYC Transit Lost Property Office (except U.S. currency, contraband, or property requiring investigation) |
| Yellow Medallion Taxis | Delivered by taxi driver to one of 8 designated commands (one per patrol borough) |

### Designated Commands for Taxi Found Property

| Borough | Command |
|---------|---------|
| PBMS | 017 Precinct |
| PBMN | Central Park Precinct |
| PBBX | 043 Precinct |
| PBBS | 076 Precinct |
| PBBN | 094 Precinct |
| PBQS | 107 Precinct |
| PBQN | 115 Precinct |
| PBSI | 120 Precinct |

### Procedure

| Step | Role | Action |
|------|------|--------|
| 1 | Member | Issue receipt to person delivering property (INVOICE copy at command; ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF FOUND PROPERTY, PD542-012, on patrol) |
| 2 | Member | Deliver subway found property to nearest NYC Transit Station Agent |
| 5 | Member | Prepare WORKSHEET |
| 9 | Desk Officer | Digitally sign INVOICE |
| 11 | Desk Officer | Have REPORT OF UNCLAIMED PROPERTY (PD542-122) prepared and forwarded |
| 12–17 | Desk Officer | If returning to owner: require valid ID, check against INVOICE, complete FORMS release |

> **Exam Alert:** Destroy all copies of REPORT OF UNCLAIMED PROPERTY if property is claimed before distribution. If claimed after distribution, forward first copy with disposition to Stolen Property Inquiry Unit.

## P.G. 218-28: Safeguarding Property of Deceased Person

**Date Effective:** 02/03/25 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 8 | **Pages:** 4

### Purpose

To safeguard property of a deceased person who lived alone, with other than a relative/competent person, or who dies at other than their residence.

### Co-Occupant Verification

A member determines if someone is a co-occupant (not visitor/guest) by:
1. Examining documents (licenses, credit cards, mail) reflecting permanent residence
2. Questioning other residents/neighbors
3. Examining closets/bureaus for clothing and possessions
4. Examining rent receipts or similar documentation
5. Considering any other relevant factors

### Patrol Supervisor Responsibilities

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 2 | Confer with Medical Examiner BEFORE directing body search |
| 3 | Obtain at least one **responsible civilian witness** when possible |
| 5 | Supervise complete search of body |
| 6 | Have all property removed from body EXCEPT clothing actually worn |
| 7 | Have clothing/evidence processed on **separate** INVOICE |
| 8 | Direct search of residence — take custody of: cash, jewelry, valuables, IDs, wills, insurance policies, bank books, safe deposit keys, prescription medication (if directed to OCME) |
| 9 | Assign member to safeguard body until removed by OCME |

> **Exam Alert:** Any uniformed member of the service may search a dead human body.

> **Exam Alert:** DO NOT seal door of premises if a **co-occupant** resided with the deceased.

> **Exam Alert:** If multiple dead human bodies as a result of a **mass casualty attack** (e.g., active shooter), do NOT search bodies for identification.

### Key Steps — Desk Officer

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 19 | Request Operations Unit to notify **Public Administrator** if no known next of kin |
| 20 | Have SEAL FOR DOOR OF D.O.A. PREMISES (PD517-120) prepared |
| 26 | Permit entry to obtain clothing for burial — direct member to accompany and replace seal |
| 34 | Refer person seeking release to **Surrogate's Court** of county where deceased lived |

## P.G. 218-30: Invoicing Property Taken from a Person's Possession

**Date Effective:** 12/31/24 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 78 | **Pages:** 2

### Purpose

To ensure persons from whom property has been taken are afforded notice as to reclaiming steps.

### Definition

**CONTRABAND** — Property whose mere possession is prohibited by law. Property is NOT contraband merely because it is suspected to be unlawfully obtained, stolen, or proceeds/instrumentality of a crime.

### Key Procedure Steps

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Prepare WORKSHEET — DO NOT enter complainant information initially |
| 2 | Allow person to examine WORKSHEET for accuracy |
| 3 | THEN enter complainant information |
| 4 | Inform person they are entitled to a copy of INVOICE; ask if they will accept |
| 5 | Inform person they may note additional/erroneous items in "Remarks" |
| 7 | Give "Prisoner/Finder/Owner" copy REGARDLESS of whether property is denominated "Arrest Evidence" |
| 8 | Provide NOTICE TO PERSONS FROM WHOM PROPERTY HAS BEEN REMOVED (PD521-124) |
| 9 | Provide VEHICLE SEIZURE FORM if vehicle involved |

> **Exam Alert:** The "Prisoner/Finder/Owner" copy MUST be given to the arrestee **prior to transporting to court section**.

> **Exam Alert:** Noncompliance with this procedure could place the Department in **contempt of court**. These provisions result from statute and court order.

## P.G. 218-35: Processing Currency Required as Evidence

**Date Effective:** 05/20/24 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 31 | **Pages:** 2

### Purpose

To record, process and safeguard currency required as evidence.

### Definition

**NUMISMATIC/SENTIMENTAL VALUE** — All gold coins, all U.S. silver coins dated 1964 or prior, and extremely old bills. If doubt exists, currency **will be deemed** to have numismatic/sentimental value. Currency with numismatic/sentimental value will **NOT** be deposited in a bank.

### Evidence Stamp

Currency is stamped "Evidence Notify N.Y.C. Police Department Property Clerk Division" EXCEPT:
- Currency with numismatic/sentimental value
- Currency that must remain unaltered (treated with dye, fluorescent powder, or marked)
- Foreign currency
- Blood-stained currency

### WORKSHEET Listing

| Type | How Listed |
|------|-----------|
| Stamped bills | Each denomination as separate item with quantity |
| Unstamped bills | Each denomination AND serial number as separate item |

> **Exam Alert:** Currency will be **perforated ONLY** by Property Clerk personnel at Property Clerk facilities.

> **Exam Alert:** Stamped or perforated currency will **NEVER** be returned to a claimant or deposited in a bank. Forward to Property Clerk for claimant reimbursement.

> **Exam Alert:** Mutilated currency (fluorescent powder, dye, blood) placed in Plastic Security Envelope marked "MUTILATED CURRENCY — CONTAINS STAINED MATERIAL — USE CARE IN HANDLING."

## P.G. 218-36: Processing Non-Evidence Currency

**Date Effective:** 12/31/24 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 78 | **Pages:** 3

### Purpose

To process currency not required as evidence (e.g., DOA property, found property).

### Key Procedure — Bank Deposit

| Step | Role | Action |
|------|------|--------|
| 6 | Desk Officer | Prepare and sign deposit slip |
| 7 | Desk Officer | Direct officer to place currency in plastic deposit bag |
| 8 | Desk Officer | Seal bag (remove protective strip, expose adhesive) |
| 14 | Desk Officer | Direct command messenger + another member to deliver to bank's **night deposit vault** at all times |
| 16 | Desk Officer | Assign second platoon member to retrieve deposit slip next business day |
| 22 | Desk Officer | Conduct "Deposit Slip Confirmation" using FORMS Property |
| 23 | Desk Officer | Notify **IAB Command Center** if discrepancy is due to possible misconduct |

### Numismatic/Sentimental Value Currency

- DO NOT enter monetary value in "Cash Value" column
- Itemize coins by quantity
- Place in Plastic Security Envelope
- Deliver to Property Clerk — DO NOT deposit in bank

> **Exam Alert:** Seizures of more than **2,000 bills** require notification to Property Clerk Division for special counting and deposit procedures.

> **Exam Alert:** All deposits are made on a "**subject to count**" basis. Bank discrepancies trigger notification from Property Clerk Division to IAB.

## P.G. 218-37: Handling of Red Dye Marked Currency and Intact "Exploding" Currency Packets

**Date Effective:** 05/20/24 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 31 | **Pages:** 2

### Intact "Exploding" Packet Found

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | DO NOT handle the packet |
| 2 | Evacuate the immediate area |
| 3 | Request patrol supervisor, ESU, and Bomb Squad |

### Handling Red Dye Currency

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 4 | Wear disposable gloves |
| 5 | Work in a ventilated area |
| 6 | Wash hands thoroughly after handling |
| 7 | Indicate on INVOICE and Plastic Security Envelope: may be contaminated with **CS Tear Gas** |

> **Exam Alert:** Do NOT open envelopes containing contaminated currency unless absolutely necessary — sealed plastic envelopes prevent dissipation of contaminated residue. Precautions apply even after **long periods of time**.

## P.G. 218-38: Processing Currency/Negotiable Instruments for Forfeiture

**Date Effective:** 12/31/24 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 78 | **Pages:** 5

### Purpose

To expeditiously process seized U.S. currency and/or negotiable instruments valued at **$1,000 or more** which may be forfeitable.

### Key Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Forfeitable** | Property used to facilitate a crime OR represents proceeds/substituted proceeds of a crime |
| **Negotiable Instrument** | Cash equivalents: money orders, traveler's checks, bank checks |
| **Substituted Proceeds** | Items received in exchange for actual proceeds of a crime |

### Key Procedure

| Step | Role | Action |
|------|------|--------|
| 1 | Member | Notify **Asset Forfeiture Unit** 24/7; request Forfeiture Log Number |
| 2 | Member | Prepare SEPARATE WORKSHEET for forfeiture currency/instruments only |
| 3 | Member | Select "FORFEITURE" as property category |
| 7 | Member | DO NOT "Evidence" stamp forfeiture currency |
| 11 | Desk Officer | Take digital photograph of currency seized per search warrant before bagging |
| 14 | Desk Officer | Direct deposit in bank's night deposit vault (two members) |

### Asset Forfeiture Unit Responsibilities

- Determine if seizure is appropriate for forfeiture
- Confer with DA's Office, Civil Enforcement Unit
- In federal cases: request adoption within **30 days**
- Property Clerk: issue check within **3 business days** upon written request for federal/state forfeiture

> **Exam Alert:** Seizures under **$1,000** do NOT require Asset Forfeiture Unit notification — no Forfeiture Log Number issued.

> **Exam Alert:** Assets will NEVER be transferred to any NYPD unit or outside agency for forfeiture processing without **written permission** of the CO, Asset Forfeiture Unit.
