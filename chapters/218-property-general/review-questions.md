# Section 218 — Review Questions

## Instructions

Select the best answer for each question. Answers are provided after each question.

---

**1.** Under P.G. 218-12, which of the following is NOT an authorized method for immobilizing a vehicle in police custody?

A) Removing rotor or coil wire and attaching to INVOICE printout
B) Disconnecting the vehicle battery
C) Utilizing wheel boots
D) Removing starter relay/fuse or fuel pump relay/fuse

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**B) Disconnecting the vehicle battery**

Members should NOT disconnect the battery due to the potential risk of damaging some vehicles. Contact the Auto Crime Unit (0800–2300) with questions. (P.G. 218-12)

</details>

---

**2.** A vehicle may be safeguarded at the command for how long when not required as evidence or forfeiture?

A) 24 hours
B) 48 hours
C) 72 hours
D) 30 days

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**B) 48 hours**

After 48 hours, the vehicle must be removed to a Property Clerk storage facility. (P.G. 218-12)

</details>

---

**3.** During an inventory search of an automobile, a locked trunk cannot be forced open UNLESS one of several exceptions applies. If the owner does not consent and no exception exists, what is required?

A) A supervisor's approval
B) A court order from the DA's office
C) A search warrant
D) Notification to the Auto Crime Unit

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**C) A search warrant**

No consent = no inventory of locked items (absent exigent circumstances). A search warrant is required. (P.G. 218-13)

</details>

---

**4.** Under the Rotation Tow program, when must the tow operator deliver a vehicle to the Property Clerk Auto Pound?

A) Within 15 days
B) Within 24 hours
C) By day 30
D) By day 60

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**C) By day 30**

Vehicles remaining at the tow facility MUST BE DELIVERED to the Auto Pound by the tow operator by day 30. (P.G. 218-21)

</details>

**5.** When serving the VEHICLE SEIZURE FORM on a defendant, the arresting officer must:

A) Have a supervisor present as witness
B) Activate the Body-Worn Camera

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

The arresting officer must activate the BWC when serving the VEHICLE SEIZURE FORM. (P.G. 218-48)

</details>

**6.** When marking firearm-related evidence, where should initials be scratched on a shell casing recovered from a crime scene?

A) The exterior of the casing
B) The bottom/base of the casing
C) The inside of the casing
D) Nowhere — shell casings are not individually marked

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**C) The inside of the casing**

Shell casings are marked on the INSIDE. DO NOT mark the exterior or bottom. (P.G. 218-23)

</details>

**7.** Firearms requiring laboratory analysis must be delivered to the patrol borough office by what time daily?

A) 2400 hours
B) 0600 hours
C) 0100 hours
D) 1800 hours

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**C) 0100 hours**

All invoiced firearms requiring Firearms Analysis Section examination must be delivered by 0100 hours daily. Delay requires exigent circumstances AND permission from the precinct CO/duty captain. (P.G. 218-23)

</details>

**8.** A PROPERTY CLERK INVOICE being sent to the Police Laboratory will not exceed how many line items?

A) 10

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**C) 20**

INVOICES sent or potentially sent to the Police Laboratory will not exceed twenty line items. (P.G. 218-01)

**9.** Which unit is the ONLY unit authorized to cancel alarms for firearms?

A) Auto Crime Unit
B) Recovering command desk officer
C) Stolen Property Inquiry Unit
D) Property Clerk Division

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**C) Stolen Property Inquiry Unit**

Cancellation of firearms alarms will be made ONLY by the Stolen Property Inquiry Unit. The recovering command will NOT cancel. (P.G. 218-23)

</details>

**10.** When seizing computer evidence, what should a member do if the computer needs to be powered off?

A) Press the on/off switch
B) Hold the power button for 10 seconds
C) Unplug the computer from the wall outlet
D) Close the laptop lid

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**C) Unplug the computer from the wall outlet**

Do NOT use the on/off switch. Unplug from the wall outlet only. Prior to removing the plug, photograph the monitor screen. (P.G. 218-31)

</details>

**11.** Invoiced property categorized as Investigatory (non-vehicle) will be disposed of by the Property Clerk after what period if no retention request is submitted?

A) 6 months
B) 1 year
C) 2 years
D) 15 days

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**B) 1 year**

The Property Clerk will dispose of investigatory property (non-vehicle) after one year from the date of invoice if no retention request is submitted. Vehicles/boats are disposed of after 15 days. (P.G. 218-55)

</details>

**12.** When processing non-evidence currency, the command messenger must be accompanied by whom when delivering the deposit bag to the bank?

A) A detective
B) The invoicing officer
C) Another uniformed member of the service or Evidence/Property Control Specialist
D) The desk officer

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

Currency is delivered to the bank's night deposit vault by the command messenger accompanied by another uniformed member or Evidence/Property Control Specialist. (P.G. 218-36)

**13.** Under P.G. 218-38, what is the minimum value of currency/negotiable instruments that requires notification to the Asset Forfeiture Unit for forfeiture processing?

A) $500
B) $1,000
C) $5,000
D) $10,000

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**B) $1,000**

Seized U.S. currency and/or negotiable instruments valued at $1,000 or more that are deemed forfeitable require notification to the Asset Forfeiture Unit (24/7). (P.G. 218-38)

</details>

**14.** When processing a deceased person's property, what must the patrol supervisor obtain before directing a body search?

A) Permission from the commanding officer
B) Conferral with the Medical Examiner
C) A search warrant
D) Written consent from next of kin

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**B) Conferral with the Medical Examiner**

The patrol supervisor must confer with the Medical Examiner or assistant Medical Examiner PRIOR to directing a member to search the body for valuables and identification documents. (P.G. 218-28)

</details>

**15.** What is the maximum number of fireworks bags that should be transported in an RMP?

A) 2 bags
B) 4 bags
C) 6 bags
D) 10 bags

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**B) 4 bags**

As a general rule, no more than four bags of fireworks should be transported in an RMP, placed in the rear seat away from heat/spark sources. (P.G. 218-53)

</details>

**18.** When the desk officer receives a request for the return of investigatory evidence, the release must be authorized or denied within how many days?

A) 5 days
B) 10 days
C) 15 days
D) 30 days

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**B) 10 days**

The desk officer must authorize release no more than ten days after receipt of the request, if property is no longer required as evidence. If denied, claimant may request CO/designee review within 5 days. (P.G. 218-02)

</details>

**19.** What is the removal fee when a licensed food vendor's vehicle, cart, or stand is removed by a Department vehicle?

A) $20.00
B) $25.00
C) $50.00
D) $65.00

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**D) $65.00**

The removal fee when property is removed by a Department vehicle is $65.00. If NOT removed by Department vehicle, the fee is $20.00. (P.G. 218-43)

**20.** When FORMS Property becomes inoperable for more than six hours and the problem is isolated to the invoicing command only, what should the desk officer instruct?

A) Use pre-printed serialized INVOICES
B) Contact the Property Clerk Division for manual processing
C) Remove property to an adjoining command and invoice there
D) Wait for the system to be restored

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

**C) Remove property to an adjoining command and invoice there**

When the outage exceeds six hours and is isolated to the command, the invoicing member removes property to an adjoining command and invoices in compliance with current procedures. The invoicing command (where the incident occurred) is selected as the invoicing command in FORMS. (P.G. 218-57)

</details>
