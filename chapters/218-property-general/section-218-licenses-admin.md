# Section 218 — Licenses and Administrative Procedures

## P.G. 218-45: Handgun License Suspension, Revocation or Cancellation

**Date Effective:** 05/20/24 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 31 | **Pages:** 3

### Purpose

To facilitate the surrender of Handgun Licenses and handguns when a license is suspended, revoked, or cancelled.

### Definition

**HANDGUN** — Any pistol or revolver listed on a Handgun License.

### Key Procedure

| Step | Role | Action |
|------|------|--------|
| 1 | Operations Coordinator | Record receipt of HANDGUN SURRENDER NOTICE (PD641-123) in Precinct Communication Log; deliver to CO |
| 2 | CO | Assign member (preferably a **supervisor**) for prompt investigation |
| 3 | CO | Ensure investigation completed and forms forwarded to License Division within **30 days** |
| 4 | Assigned Member | Respond to licensee's address; request surrender of license and handgun(s) |
| 4a | Assigned Member | Attempt visits at times other than second platoon |
| 4b | Assigned Member | Contact License Division if licensee refuses AND offers no evidence of legal disposal |
| 6 | Assigned Member | Complete HANDGUN SURRENDER NOTICE (3 copies: Original–precinct, Copy 1–License Division, Copy 2–licensee) |
| 7 | Assigned Member | Prepare INVOICE; process per P.G. 218-23 |
| 7a | Assigned Member | Include in Remarks: "HANDGUNS SHALL NOT BE RELEASED WITHOUT WRITTEN AUTHORIZATION OF THE COMMANDING OFFICER, LICENSE DIVISION" |
| 8 | Assigned Member | Attempt to obtain new address if licensee relocated |
| 9 | Assigned Member | Prepare COMPLAINT REPORT and refer to detective squad when: handgun lost/stolen, licensee avoiding service, or handguns may have been used in crime |

### Legal Disposal

A licensee may legally dispose of a properly registered handgun by:
1. Sale to a licensed NYS gun dealer
2. Sale to a person authorized to possess handguns
3. Surrendering for safekeeping to a police agency

> **Exam Alert:** Investigation must be completed within **30 days** of receipt of HANDGUN SURRENDER NOTICE.

> **Sergeant Focus:** CO should assign a **supervisor** to conduct the investigation.

## P.G. 218-55: Retention of Invoiced Property

**Date Effective:** 12/31/24 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 78 | **Pages:** 2

### Purpose

To properly record retention requests for invoiced property.

### Retention Deadlines

| Property Type | Deadline | Action If Missed |
|--------------|----------|-----------------|
| Property (non-vehicle/boat) | **1 year** from date of invoice | Property Clerk disposes |
| Motor vehicle/boat | **15 days** from date of invoice | Property Clerk disposes |

### Exceptions to Automatic Disposal

Property involved in:
- Homicide investigations
- Sexual offenses
- Arson/explosion investigations
- Member of service involved cases
- IAB cases

### Procedure (FORMS Property)

| Step | Role | Action |
|------|------|--------|
| 1 | Assigned Investigator/Invoicing Officer | Prepare REQUEST FOR RETENTION (PD521-169) using FORMS Property |
| 2 | Same | Prepare **separate** REQUEST for each associated INVOICE |
| 3 | Property Clerk Facility | Review; if approved, digitally sign and retain; if disapproved, provide explanation |

> **Exam Alert:** Each retention request retains property for **one year only**. A new REQUEST FOR RETENTION must be submitted for each additional one-year period.

> **Exam Alert:** Failure to submit retention request within 1 year (property) or 15 days (vehicle/boat) = Property Clerk **disposes of the property** (except homicide, sex offense, arson, MOS/IAB cases).

## P.G. 218-56: Change of Category for Invoiced Property

**Date Effective:** 12/31/24 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 78 | **Pages:** 3

### Purpose

To properly record changes to the category of invoiced property.

### When Category Change Is Required

| Circumstance | Category Change |
|-------------|----------------|
| Case Closed / Arrest | Investigatory → **Arrest Evidence** (or DNA Investigatory → DNA Arrest Evidence) — required for Property Clerk to retain |
| Case Closed / No Arrest | Investigatory → **Safekeeping** |
| Other instances | As applicable |

### Procedure (FORMS Property)

| Step | Role | Action |
|------|------|--------|
| 1 | Assigned Investigator/Invoicing Officer | Use "Change of Category" function; complete REQUEST FOR INVOICE CHANGE OF CATEGORY (PD521-167) |
| 2 | Same | Prepare **separate** REQUEST for each associated INVOICE |
| 3 | Desk Officer/Supervisor at Property Clerk Facility | Review and digitally sign — approve or reject |

> **Exam Alert:** If property still at command (snap-out INVOICE), the original INVOICE is **voided** and property re-invoiced in the correct category using FORMS Property.

> **Exam Alert:** After category change, Property Clerk determines if property is now **eligible for disposal**.

## P.G. 218-57: Invoicing Property Due to FORMS Application Failure

**Date Effective:** 12/31/24 | **Last Revision:** R.O. 78 | **Pages:** 2

### Purpose

To set guidelines for invoicing property when FORMS Property becomes inoperable.

### Procedure by Outage Duration

| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| **6 hours or less** | Prepare WORKSHEET; enter into FORMS when it becomes operational |
| **More than 6 hours, command-only problem** | Remove property to **adjoining command** and invoice there |
| **Citywide outage, more than 6 hours** | Contact ITB Service Desk supervisor; follow supervisor's instructions |

### Notifications

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Immediately notify **Information Technology Bureau (ITB) Service Desk** |
| 2 | Ascertain: when correctable, command-only or borough/citywide, anticipated duration |
| 3 | Command Log entry: ITB member notified, Service Desk ticket number, anticipated duration |

### Adjoining Command Invoicing

- Select **command where incident occurred** as invoicing command (not adjoining command)
- During "Putaway" function, select the **invoicing command** as storage location

> **Exam Alert:** Pre-printed serialized INVOICES stored at commands are NOT to be used until instructed by the CO, Information Technology Systems Division.

> **Sergeant Focus:** COs must ensure adequate supply of pre-printed WORKSHEETS, INVOICES, and associated forms are stored in the property room for use during FORMS outages.
