**1. Who is required to use a paper Activity Log (PD112-145) instead of the digital Activity Log?**

**6. What distinguishes a "reach train" from a "rescue train"?**

- C) Reach trains require power removal; rescue trains do not

**2. Which of the following persons is NOT authorized to disturb a dead body at a scene?**

- A. A member of the Emergency Service Unit, if the body is on train tracks
- B. A member of Highway District assigned as a collision technician
- C. The patrol supervisor directing the investigation
- D. The Medical Examiner or assistant Medical Examiner
