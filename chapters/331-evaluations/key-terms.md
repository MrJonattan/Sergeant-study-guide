## PERF System Annual Score Conversion (Non-Investigative)

| Total Annual Score | Overall Evaluation |
|---|---|
| **90-100** | Exceptional |
| **77-89** | Exceeds Expectations |
| **68-76** | Meets Standards |
| **60-67** | Needs Improvement |
| **59 or below** | Unsatisfactory |

| Needs improvement/unsatisfactory (67 or lower) — 331-04 | Captain or above |

## Chronic Absent Impact on Evaluations

| Category | Evaluation Guidance |
|----------|-------------------|
| Chronic Absent - Category A | Give consideration to attendance record in determining overall rating |
| Chronic Absent - Category B | Consider evaluating "Very Low" (331-01) or "Unsatisfactory" (331-04) |
| Category B (investigative) | Presumed substandard; if rated higher than "Low," rater must justify |

## Military Absence Rule

- Member still entitled to a Performance Evaluation
- Rating = **average of 3 prior evaluations**
- Credited rating must not be less than **satisfactory (3.0)** or less than the rating immediately prior to military absence
- Rater notes military duty period in "Overall Rater's Comments"

## Key Distinctions

| Concept | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Appeal rights | Probationary members and civilians with < 2 years provisional status **cannot appeal** |
| Probation extension request | Made after **6 months** by CO, with **4th month evaluation**, forwarded to Deputy Commissioner, Risk Management |
| Evaluation prepared in error | Submit **Typed Letterhead** to Performance Analysis Unit to void/delete |
| Precinct detective squad supervisor eval | Rater must **confer with precinct CO** and note in Rater Comments |
| Transfer evaluation | Must be prepared; forwarded to **new command** |
| Rated by command on | **Last day of rating period** |
