# Section 208 — Notification Requirements

Covers P.G. 208-69

---

## P.G. 208-69: Notifications

### Operations Unit — DIRECT Notification Required

The following arrest types require **direct notification to the Operations Unit**:

| Category | Specific Situations |
|---|---|
| **Alcohol** | Illegal alcohol manufacture |
| **Food stamps** | Federal food stamps valued at **>$500** |
| **Government employees** | NYC, federal, or state employee arrests |
| **Members of the Service** | Any MOS arrest |
| **Tax offenses** | Cigarette tax violations |
| **Prescription fraud** | Forged narcotics prescriptions |
| **Tax-related crimes** | Extortion or bribery related to taxes |
| **Identity documents** | Multiple blank or different Social Security cards |
| **Parking meters** | Parking meter larceny |
| **Postal violations** | Any postal-related offense |
| **Military** | Armed Forces personnel, AWOL, or deserter |
| **Health impersonation** | DOH (Department of Health) impersonation |

> **Exam Alert:** The $500 threshold for federal food stamps is a frequently tested number. MOS arrests ALWAYS require Operations Unit notification.

### Direct Notification to Specialized Units

| Unit to Notify | Trigger Situations |
|---|---|
| **Intelligence Division** | Criminal anarchy, anarchistic literature, UN employee arrest, foreign armed forces member, youth gang activity |
| **Port Authority** | Offenses involving Port Authority jurisdiction |
| **TBTA** (Triborough Bridge and Tunnel Authority) | Offenses involving TBTA facilities |
| **NYS DOCCS** (Dept of Corrections and Community Supervision) | Parole violator arrests |
| **NYS Police** | Offenses requiring state police notification |
| **NYS Court System** | Offenses involving court personnel or on court property |
| **School Safety Division** | Offenses involving **DOE** (Department of Education) facilities or personnel |
| **Citywide Vandals Task Force** | **Graffiti** offenses |
| **CUNY** | Offenses involving City University of New York facilities or personnel |

> **Sergeant Focus:** Supervisors must ensure all required notifications are made promptly. Failure to notify specialized units can compromise investigations and inter-agency relationships. The Intelligence Division notification list is particularly important — UN employees, foreign armed forces, and criminal anarchy all require immediate Intelligence Division contact.
