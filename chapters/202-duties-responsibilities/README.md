## Learning Objectives
- Understand the specific duties and responsibilities assigned to each rank within the patrol function
- Know the Desk Officer's duties regarding command administration and supervision
- Identify the Patrol Supervisor's responsibilities for roll call, patrol, and field supervision
- Recognize the distinction between duties that are mandatory ("shall") versus discretionary ("may")

## Sergeant Focus Areas
- Mastering all Patrol Supervisor duties including roll call inspections and field supervision
- Understanding Desk Officer responsibilities and when to assume that role
- Ensuring proper tour coverage, meal assignments, and post assignments
- Supervising subordinates' adherence to their assigned duties and responsibilities
- NST and CRT supervision requirements (solely assigned sergeant, BWC review, CRAFT forms)
- Critical numbers: 2-hour AWOL, 5-minute roll call, 5th-of-month profile reports, 4th-hour email, 5-block response, 3-per-vehicle, 5 NST max
