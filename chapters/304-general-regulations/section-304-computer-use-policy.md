# Section 304 — Department Computer Use Policy (A.G. 325-35)

## A.G. 325-35: Department Computer Use Policy and Monitoring Notice

**Date Effective:** 02/04/15 | **Pages:** 1

> **Note:** This procedure is from A.G. Section 325 (Department Property) but is included here with General Regulations due to its conduct/policy nature.

### Purpose

To inform members of the service of the Department computer use policy when accessing computer systems, and to notify all members that the Department reserves and will exercise the right to review, intercept, access, and disclose all material in the Department's computer systems at any time, with or without notice.

### Procedure

| Role | Step | Action |
|------|------|--------|
| Commanding Officer | 1 | Inform member concerned of the Department Computer Use Policy and Monitoring Notice |
| Member | 2 | Review and comply with contents of the Policy and Monitoring Notice |

### Department Computer Use Policy — Key Rules

| Rule |
|------|
| All computer systems and equipment are intended for **official Department business only** |
| Use for **personal or non-Department business** is strictly prohibited — subject to disciplinary action |
| Use to disseminate **derogatory or discriminatory material** is strictly prohibited |
| Department reserves and will exercise the right to **review, intercept, access, and disclose** all material at any time, **with or without notice** |
| Access may occur **during or after** the user's assigned tours |
