Intoxicated UMOS — Key Steps

1. Direct member to remain at location 2. Prepare PD469-150 IMMEDIATELY (both supervisor AND CO/XO/duty captain) 3. Remove firearms (A.G. 318-13) 4. Modified assignment or suspend 5. Mandated contact to Counseling Services Unit 6. Notify IAB Command Center — obtain log number 7. 8 copies of Typed Letterhead to 8 recipients
NOTE: Firearm + alcohol = TERMINATION | Motor vehicle + serious injury + alcohol = TERMINATION
