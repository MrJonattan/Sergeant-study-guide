Force Incident Levels

Level Key Criteria Investigated By
1 Hand/foot strikes, takedowns, O.C. spray, CEW cartridge, physical injury from police action Immediate Supervisor
2 Striking with object/vehicle, canine bite, CEW drive stun, prohibited acts (excl. chokehold), excessive force allegation, unconscious/stitches/lost tooth CO/XO/Duty Captain + IAB
3 Force capable of causing death/serious injury (not firearm), chokehold, excessive force + serious injury Internal Affairs Bureau
4 ALL firearms discharges, UMOS firearm fired by another, subject dies/seriously injured in custody Force Investigation Division

Immediate Supervisor = NOT involved, same command, one rank higher than all involved members

BWC Rules: Level 1-2 → supervisor reviews | Level 3-4 → DO NOT view — safeguard, await IAB/FID

Intoxicated UMOS — Key Steps

  1. Direct member to remain at location
  2. Prepare PD469-150 IMMEDIATELY (both supervisor AND CO/XO/duty captain)
  3. Remove firearms (A.G. 318-13)
  4. Modified assignment or suspend
  5. Mandated contact to Counseling Services Unit
  6. Notify IAB Command Center — obtain log number
  7. 8 copies of Typed Letterhead to 8 recipients

Firearm + alcohol = TERMINATION | Motor vehicle + serious injury + alcohol = TERMINATION
