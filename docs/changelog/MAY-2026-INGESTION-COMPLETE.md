## 🔍 Key Content Added

### 212-57: Service of Family Court/Supreme Court Orders of Protection

**Key Points:**
- Desk officer **must accept** orders from public (accepting ≠ serving)
- NYC Sheriff's Office is process server during operating hours
- **Fax hours:** Mon-Thu 0300-2300, Fri 0500-2300
- Order of Protection Log maintained at desk

### 214-34: Police Action in Premises Where Alcoholic Beverages Are Sold

**Key Points:**
- Applies to arrests, summonses, complaints, conditions at licensed/unlicensed premises
- Patrol supervisor **must respond** to scene
- Notify Detective Bureau Operations Desk for unlicensed sales or after-hours operations
- PD310-150 report required

### 208-14: Arrest by Uniformed Member Outside City

**Key Points:**
- Applies when not specifically assigned or ordered
- Notify Operations Unit **immediately**
- Duty captain responds if within residence counties
- Operations Unit decides for outside residence counties

### 218-26: Processing Found Property

**Key Points:**
- Found property **$10+** must be reported/deposited
- Subway found property → NYC Transit Lost Property Office
- Taxi found property → 8 designated repository commands
- Issue receipts (PD521-141 at command, PD542-012 on patrol)
