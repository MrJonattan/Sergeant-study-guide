## Force Incident Levels

| Level | Key Criteria | Investigated By |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| **1** | Hand/foot strikes, takedowns, O.C. spray, CEW **cartridge**, physical injury from police action | **Immediate Supervisor** |
| **2** | Striking with object/vehicle, canine bite, CEW **drive stun**, prohibited acts (excl. chokehold), excessive force allegation, unconscious/stitches/lost tooth | **CO/XO/Duty Captain + IAB** |
| **3** | Force capable of causing death/serious injury (not firearm), chokehold, excessive force + serious injury | **Internal Affairs Bureau** |
| **4** | ALL firearms discharges, UMOS firearm fired by another, subject dies/seriously injured in custody | **Force Investigation Division** |

> **NOTE:** Immediate Supervisor = NOT involved, same command, **one rank higher** than all involved members

> **NOTE:** BWC Rules: Level 1-2 → supervisor reviews | Level 3-4 → **DO NOT view** — safeguard, await IAB/FID

|--------|------------|----------------|

> **NOTE:** NEVER use O.C., CEW, or impact weapons on passively resisting subjects
> **NOTE:** NEVER use CEW on rear-cuffed prisoner
> **NOTE:** CEW holster: Support side, opposite firearm — no cross-draw

## Retirement Quick Reference

| Item | Tier 2 | Tier 3 |
|------|--------|--------|
| PPF advance notice | **30 days** | No notice required |
| Mandatory retirement | Eve of **63rd** birthday | Eve of **62nd** birthday |
| Retired ID/LEOSA timing | At discontinuance | **30 days** after (unless 30 days advance to PPF) |

> **NOTE:** Terminal Leave = 1 month per 10 years + 3 days/year beyond 20 + 1 day per 4 months
> **NOTE:** LEOSA = 20+ years or LOD injury, qualified within 12 months, good standing
> **Memory Aid — TIER 2 vs TIER 3 RETIREMENT:** Tier 2 = **63**rd birthday (higher age, "2" has **3** digits); Tier 3 = **62**nd birthday (lower age, "3" has **2** digits)
> **Memory Aid — TIER 2 vs TIER 3 PPF NOTICE:** Tier 2 = 30 days advance ("Two = Takes Time"). Tier 3 = No notice required ("Three = Free").
> **Memory Aid — TERMINAL LEAVE MATH:** 1 month per 10 years + 3 days per year beyond 20 + 1 day per 4 months partial year. Example: 23 years = 2 months (20 years) + 9 days (3 × 3) = 2 months + 9 days.
> **Memory Aid — FIREARMS UPON RESIGNATION:** Deliver to **borough Property Clerk's Office ONLY**. LEOSA holders don't deliver firearms.

## Intoxicated UMOS — Key Steps

1. Direct member to remain at location
2. Prepare **PD469-150 IMMEDIATELY** (both supervisor AND CO/XO/duty captain)
3. Remove firearms (A.G. 318-13)
4. Modified assignment or suspend
5. **Mandated** contact to Counseling Services Unit
6. Notify **IAB Command Center** — obtain log number
7. 8 copies of Typed Letterhead to 8 recipients

> **NOTE:** Firearm + alcohol = TERMINATION | Motor vehicle + serious injury + alcohol = TERMINATION
