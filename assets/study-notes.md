## Sergeant-Specific Responsibilities

### Must Respond To Scene
- Vehicle pursuits (P.G. 221-15)
- Force incidents Level 1-2 (P.G. 221-03)
- Firearms discharge (P.G. 221-04)
- EDP incidents (P.G. 221-13)
- Death in custody (P.G. 221-05)

### Must Notify Immediately
- IAB Command Center (suspensions, force incidents)
- Duty Captain (ongoing pursuits, critical incidents)
- Borough Commander (certain deaths, officer-involved shootings)
- CCRB (civilian complaints within 7 days)

### Must Review/Sign
- Activity Logs (daily review)
- T.R.I. reports (before end of tour)
- Vehicle Pursuit Report (within 24 hours)
- PD469-150 (Fitness for Duty — both supervisor AND CO prepare separately)

## Exam Traps

1. **"Shall" vs "Should"** — "Shall" = mandatory, "Should" = discretionary
2. **"Immediately"** — Without delay, but allows for completing urgent tasks first
3. **"When feasible"** — Only if practical under circumstances
4. **"As necessary"** — Sergeant discretion based on situation
5. **"Absent extraordinary circumstances"** — Only Police Commissioner can determine
